## Post 3: Behind the Scenes — Engine Analysis

**Post Type:** Text + Image

**Text:**
```
Behind the scenes: How I analyze positions for videos

Every famous game breakdown on this channel goes through the same process:

1. I play through the game manually first, writing down my own thoughts at each critical moment
2. Then I run the position through Pikafish (the strongest xiangqi engine in the world) at depth 30+
3. I compare what the masters played vs. what the engine recommends
4. The most interesting videos happen when the master plays a move the engine hates — but it WORKS

The gap between human intuition and engine calculation is where the best stories live.

What is Pikafish? It is an open-source xiangqi engine based on Stockfish architecture, currently rated over 3000 ELO. Stronger than any human player in history.

Would you like to see a dedicated "Engine vs. Grandmaster" series? Let me know in the comments.
```
