# YouTube Channel Trailer Script (60 Seconds)

**Format:** 16:9 landscape, 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
**Voice:** MiniMax TTS — Male, authoritative but warm, moderate pace
**Music:** Epic orchestral build (0:00-0:30), transitions to warm/inviting (0:30-1:00)
**Subtitles:** Hardcoded — Chinese top line, English bottom line, white text with dark shadow

---

## [0:00-0:05] HOOK — The Move That Changed History

**[VISUAL]** Close-up of a xiangqi board. A hand reaches in and moves a cannon. The camera zooms into the piece as it slides into position. Screen flash on impact.

**[VOICEOVER — Chinese]**
"一步棋，改变历史。"

**[SUBTITLE — English]**
"One move changed history."

**[MiniMax Note]** Dramatic pause after "历史." Use the "Narration" voice preset. Slow delivery — approximately 1.5 seconds of speech, 1 second of silence before next section. Set emotion parameter to "serious."

## [0:05-0:15] WHAT — What This Channel Is About

**[VISUAL]** Quick cuts: a board diagram animating a famous sacrifice, engine evaluation bar swinging from -3 to +7, a hand writing Chinese notation on screen. Each cut is 2-3 seconds.

**[VOICEOVER — Chinese]**
"这个频道，专门拆解中国象棋历史上最精彩的妙手。每一步棋背后的思考，每一个局面隐藏的杀招——我们一个一个讲透。"

**[SUBTITLE — English]**
"This channel breaks down the most brilliant moves in Chinese chess history. The thinking behind every move, the hidden tactics in every position — we explain them all."

**[MiniMax Note]** Slightly faster pace here — conversational energy. Emphasis on "最精彩" (most brilliant) and "讲透" (explain thoroughly). Use "Conversational" voice preset.

## [0:15-0:30] SHOW — Quick Montage of Content Types

**[VISUAL]** Three distinct clips, ~5 seconds each:

**Clip 1 (0:15-0:20) — Puzzle Reveal:**
Board position on screen. Text overlay: "你会走吗？" (Would you play this?). Pause. Then the winning move animates in with a satisfying click sound. Text: "马后炮！" with a burst animation.

**Clip 3 (0:25-0:30) — Engine vs Grandmaster:**
Screen recording style: Pikafish engine evaluation running. The bar swings dramatically. Text overlay: "引擎说这步是错的——但大师赢了" (The engine says this move is wrong — but the master won).

**[VOICEOVER — Chinese]**
"残局谜题，让你动脑。名局拆解，让你涨棋。引擎对决大师，让你开眼。"

**[SUBTITLE — English]**
"Endgame puzzles to challenge your mind. Famous game breakdowns to level up your play. Engine versus grandmaster to blow your mind."

**[MiniMax Note]** Three parallel phrases — deliver with rhythmic cadence, slight pause between each. This is the "power trio" moment. Use "Narration" preset with pace set to 1.1x.

## Production Notes

### MiniMax TTS Settings Summary
| Section | Preset | Pace | Emotion |
|---------|--------|------|---------|
| Hook | Narration | 0.85x | Serious/dramatic |
| What | Conversational | 1.0x | Confident |
| Show | Narration | 1.1x | Rhythmic/energetic |
| Why | Conversational | 1.0x | Warm/inclusive |
| Proof | Energetic | 1.15x | Excited |
| CTA | Warm | 0.9x | Sincere |

### Audio Layers
1. **Voiceover** — MiniMax TTS, exported as WAV, noise-reduced in Premiere
2. **Background music** — Royalty-free epic/cinematic, ducked -12dB under voice
3. **SFX** — Chess piece clicks (3 instances), whoosh transitions (4 instances), bell ring (1 instance at CTA)

### Export Settings
- Upload as channel trailer (set in YouTube Studio > Customization > Layout > Video spotlight > "For new visitors")
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Audio: AAC 256kbps stereo
- Total file target: under 50MB

### Thumbnail (for trailer)
Not strictly required (auto-plays on channel page), but set a custom thumbnail:
- Red and black xiangqi board, dramatic lighting
- Text: "象棋 — 从入门到精通" / "Chinese Chess — Beginner to Master"
- Channel logo in corner
