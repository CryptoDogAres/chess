## Product Categories by Difficulty & Investment

### Easy: Digital Products (Low risk, high margin, immediate launch)

These require only time to create. No inventory, no shipping, no upfront cost.

**1. PDF Puzzle Books**
- Collections of 50-100 puzzles per book, organized by theme
- Themes: Checkmate in 1, Checkmate in 2, Checkmate in 3, Tactical Combinations, Endgame Studies
- Include board diagrams, FEN strings, and full solutions with explanations
- Price: $5-$10 per book
- Create with LaTeX or Canva for professional formatting
- Sell via Gumroad, Payhip, or direct link on 小鹅通

**2. FEN Position Packs**
- Curated sets of 200+ training positions in FEN format
- Categories: Opening positions to study, Critical middlegame decisions, Winning endgame technique
- Compatible with any xiangqi software or engine
- Price: $3-$5 per pack
- Practically zero creation cost if positions are collected during content creation

**3. Digital Wallpapers & Artwork**
- Artistic xiangqi board positions from famous games
- Calligraphy-style piece characters (車馬炮) in various designs
- Desktop, phone, and tablet sizes
- Price: $2-$3 per set, or free with email signup (lead generation)
- Commission from a graphic designer on Fiverr ($50-$100 per set of designs)

**4. Printable Study Sheets**
- One-page reference cards: piece values, opening move orders, checkmate patterns
- Designed for printing and laminating
- Price: $1-$2 each, or bundle of 10 for $8
- Free versions drive traffic; premium versions have detailed annotations

**5. Opening Repertoire Files**
- Complete opening databases in PGN-equivalent format for xiangqi
- Organized by system: 中炮 Complete, 飞相局 Complete, 反宫马 Complete
- Annotated with explanations at key decision points
- Price: $8-$12 per system

### Medium: Print-on-Demand (No inventory risk, moderate margin)

Print-on-demand (POD) services handle printing, shipping, and returns. You upload designs and collect royalties.

**6. T-Shirts**
- Designs featuring xiangqi themes (see design concepts below)
- Use Redbubble, Teespring, or Printful
- Price: $22-$28 (you earn $5-$10 per shirt)
- Start with 3-5 designs, expand based on sales data

**7. Mugs**
- Xiangqi piece artwork, funny xiangqi quotes, position diagrams
- Popular as gifts for chess-playing friends and family
- Price: $15-$18 (you earn $3-$6 per mug)

**8. Phone Cases**
- Clean piece character designs work well on cases
- Offer for iPhone and major Android models
- Price: $18-$22 (you earn $4-$7 per case)

**9. Posters & Prints**
- Famous game positions rendered as art prints
- Historical xiangqi artwork reproductions
- Price: $12-$20 (you earn $3-$8 per print)

**10. Stickers & Patches**
- Low price point, impulse buy
- Individual piece characters, funny xiangqi phrases
- Price: $3-$5 (you earn $1-$2 per sticker pack)

### Hard: Physical Products (Higher investment, higher perceived value)

These require upfront capital, inventory management, and shipping logistics.

**11. Branded Xiangqi Sets**
- Partner with a manufacturer (淘宝/1688 sourcing) for custom sets with channel branding
- Options: acrylic pieces, wooden pieces, magnetic travel sets
- Minimum order quantities typically 100-500 units
- Price: $25-$80 depending on material quality
- Margin: 40-60% after manufacturing and shipping costs
- Start with a single high-quality design, test demand before scaling

**12. Printed Instruction Books**
- Professional-quality printed version of course content
- Publish through Amazon KDP (print-on-demand, no inventory) or traditional Chinese publishing
- English and Chinese versions
- Price: $15-$25 per book
- Consider this after the paid courses prove successful — the content already exists

**13. Custom Chess Clocks**
- Branded digital chess clocks with xiangqi-specific time control presets
- High perceived value for tournament players
- Price: $30-$50
- Small market but high loyalty — serious players appreciate specialized tools

## 10 Merchandise Design Concepts

### 1. 马后炮 (Horse-Cannon Checkmate)
A stylized illustration of the classic checkmate pattern: horse controlling the escape square, cannon delivering the final blow. Bold red and black color scheme. Text: "马后炮" in brushstroke calligraphy. Works on t-shirts, mugs, and posters.

### 2. 楚河汉界 (River Border)
Minimalist design of the xiangqi board's central river with the characters 楚河 and 汉界 in elegant calligraphy. Clean horizontal layout perfect for t-shirts (across the chest) and desktop wallpapers. Historical and artistic appeal.

### 3. The Seven Pieces (七子)
All seven piece types (將/帥, 車, 馬, 炮, 仕, 相, 兵/卒) arranged in a circular or artistic layout. Each piece shown in its traditional circular design with the Chinese character. Works as a poster, phone case, or sticker set (individual pieces sold separately).

### 4. "I Sacrifice My Chariot" (弃车攻杀)
Humorous design showing a chariot piece being tossed aside with a knowing smirk. Text options: "Sometimes you have to sacrifice your best piece" (English) or "弃车妙手" (Chinese). Appeals to players who appreciate tactical boldness. T-shirt and mug design.

### 5. Engine Says +9.7
A screen mockup showing Pikafish evaluation of a blunder, with the evaluation bar showing massive advantage. Relatable humor for anyone who has analyzed their games. Text: "Pikafish says: +9.7. I played this position and lost." T-shirt and sticker design.

### 6. Red vs. Black Minimalist
Two overlapping circles — one red, one black — each containing their respective general character (帅/将). Clean, modern, minimal. Works on everything: phone cases, t-shirts, mugs, stickers, wallpapers.

### 7. Opening Tree (开局谱)
A branching tree diagram showing the major opening systems (中炮, 飞相, 起马, 仙人指路) as branches, with variations as smaller branches. Stylized as a literal tree with Chinese characters as leaves. Poster and print format — educational and decorative.

### 8. 观棋不语 (Watch Chess in Silence)
The classic Chinese saying about not giving advice while watching others play. Rendered in bold calligraphy with a small illustration of two players and a frustrated spectator biting their tongue. Humorous cultural reference. T-shirt and mug design.

### 9. Position of the Day
A rotating concept: famous game positions rendered as clean diagrams with minimal annotation. Sold as a poster series or calendar. Each position includes a QR code linking to the analysis video. Bridges merchandise and content.

### 10. 将军 (Check!)
The word 将军 in dramatic brushstroke calligraphy, with ink splatter effects suggesting the intensity of the attack. Red ink on black background or black ink on white. Simple but striking. Works on t-shirts, phone cases, and stickers.

## Pricing Strategy

### Digital Products
- Price based on content depth, not production cost
- Puzzle books: $5 for 50 puzzles, $10 for 100 puzzles (10 cents per puzzle)
- Position packs: $3-$5 (bulk content, utility-focused)
- Wallpapers: $2-$3 or free with email signup
- Opening files: $8-$12 (high perceived value for serious players)

### POD Products
- Set markup to earn $5-$10 per item (shirts, mugs, cases)
- Keep total price competitive: t-shirts under $28, mugs under $18
- Offer periodic discount codes (10-15%) for community members
- Never discount below a $3 profit margin per item

### Physical Products
- Price at 2-2.5x the total cost (manufacturing + shipping + platform fees)
- Chess sets: $25 budget, $50 standard, $80 premium
- Books: $15-$25 based on page count and production quality

### Bundle Strategies
- "Puzzle Master Bundle" — all 5 puzzle books for $35 (30% savings)
- "Complete Digital Collection" — all digital products for $50 (40%+ savings)
- "Merch + Course" — t-shirt + Tier 1 course for $45 (save $6)

## Integration with Video Content

### Natural Mention Guidelines

The goal is to mention merchandise in a way that adds value to the viewer, not interrupt their experience. Follow these principles:

**1. Wear your own merch in videos.**
Wear xiangqi-themed t-shirts during recordings. Viewers will notice and ask about them in comments. Pin a comment with the store link. This is the least intrusive promotion method.

**2. Use puzzle books as content hooks.**
"This position is from my 100 Checkmate Puzzles book — if you want more like this, the link is in the description. Let me show you the solution."

**3. Feature the branded chess set.**
Use the branded physical set in any over-the-board content. Mention it once when it first appears: "I am using our custom xiangqi set today — you can find it linked below." Do not repeat in every video.

**4. End-screen merch spotlight.**
Dedicate the last 10 seconds of select videos (not every video) to a specific product. Rotate which product is featured. "If you enjoyed today's puzzle, you will love the tactical combinations puzzle book — 100 positions to sharpen your skills."

**5. Community integration.**
Post in Discord/QQ: "New design dropped — 马后炮 t-shirt available now." Include a mockup image. Community members who feel part of the brand are the most likely buyers.

**6. Limited editions.**
Release time-limited designs tied to events: tournament season, Chinese New Year, channel milestones. Scarcity drives purchases. "This design is only available until February 15th."

**7. Giveaways.**
Give away 1-2 items per month in community contests (puzzle challenges, best game submissions). Winners become walking advertisements. Cost: $15-$30/month. Value: social proof and engagement.
