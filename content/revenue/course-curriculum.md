## Tier 1: 入门班 Beginner ($29)

**Target audience:** Complete beginners, casual players who learned informally, international chess players crossing over.

**Goal:** Student can play a complete game correctly and understand basic strategy.

### Lesson List

**1. 棋盘与棋子 Board & Pieces**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Identify all 7 piece types and their Chinese characters; understand the board layout including the river and palace; set up a board from scratch
- Practice exercises: 5
- Prerequisite: None

**2. 车的走法 How the Chariot Moves**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Move the chariot correctly in all directions; understand why the chariot is the strongest piece; capture enemy pieces with the chariot
- Practice exercises: 6
- Prerequisite: Lesson 1

**3. 马的走法与蹩马腿 Horse Movement & Blocking**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Trace the horse's L-shaped path; identify blocking pieces (蹩马腿) in any position; compare to international chess knight differences
- Practice exercises: 8
- Prerequisite: Lesson 1

**4. 炮的走法与吃子 Cannon Movement & Capture**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Move the cannon along straight lines; capture by jumping over exactly one piece (炮架); recognize when a cannon can and cannot capture
- Practice exercises: 8
- Prerequisite: Lesson 1

**5. 兵与卒 Soldiers / Pawns**
- Duration: 10 min
- Objectives: Move pawns forward before crossing the river; move pawns forward or sideways after crossing; understand why crossed-river pawns gain value
- Practice exercises: 4
- Prerequisite: Lesson 1

**6. 仕与相 Advisors & Elephants**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Move advisors diagonally within the palace; move elephants in their fixed diagonal pattern; identify elephant blocking (蹩象眼)
- Practice exercises: 6
- Prerequisite: Lesson 1

**7. 将与帅 The Generals (Kings)**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Move the general within the palace; understand the face-to-face rule (对面笑); recognize check and checkmate
- Practice exercises: 5
- Prerequisite: Lessons 1-6

**8. 将军、应将、将死 Check, Respond, Checkmate**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Identify all forms of check; list the three responses to check (block, capture, move); determine if a position is checkmate or stalemate
- Practice exercises: 10
- Prerequisite: Lessons 1-7

**9. 基本规则与禁手 Rules & Forbidden Moves**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Understand perpetual check rules; recognize forbidden perpetual chase; know the draw conditions
- Practice exercises: 5
- Prerequisite: Lesson 8

**10. 你的第一盘棋 Your First Complete Game**
- Duration: 20 min
- Objectives: Play a full game from start to finish; apply all piece movement rules correctly; practice making captures and responding to checks
- Practice exercises: 1 (full game)
- Prerequisite: Lessons 1-9

**11. 子力价值 Piece Values**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Rank all pieces by approximate value; evaluate simple trades (is chariot for horse + cannon fair?); understand how piece values shift in endgames
- Practice exercises: 6
- Prerequisite: Lesson 10

**12. 开局基本原则 Opening Principles**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Develop chariots early; control the center with the cannon; avoid moving the same piece twice without reason
- Practice exercises: 4
- Prerequisite: Lesson 11

**13. 中炮开局入门 Introduction to Center Cannon Opening**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Play the center cannon opening (炮二平五) correctly; understand the first 4-5 moves; recognize common opponent responses
- Practice exercises: 5
- Prerequisite: Lesson 12

**14. 仕角炮与过宫炮 Advisor-Angle & Palace Cannon**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Play two alternative cannon openings; understand when to choose each; compare strengths and weaknesses to center cannon
- Practice exercises: 4
- Prerequisite: Lesson 12

**15. 简单杀法：白脸将 Basic Checkmates: Face-to-Face**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Set up the face-to-face general checkmate; recognize the pattern from 2-3 moves away; use this in endgames with material advantage
- Practice exercises: 8
- Prerequisite: Lesson 8

**16. 简单杀法：马后炮 Basic Checkmates: Horse-Cannon Mate**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Understand the horse-cannon checkmate pattern; position the horse and cannon correctly; execute the mate in practice positions
- Practice exercises: 8
- Prerequisite: Lesson 15

**17. 简单杀法：双车错 Basic Checkmates: Double Chariot**
- Duration: 12 min
- Objectives: Coordinate two chariots for checkmate; use alternating checks to drive the general; convert chariot advantage to checkmate
- Practice exercises: 6
- Prerequisite: Lesson 15

**18. 简单残局：车胜单仕 Basic Endgame: Chariot vs. Lone Advisor**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Win with chariot against a lone advisor; use the general to assist the attack; avoid stalemate traps
- Practice exercises: 5
- Prerequisite: Lesson 17

**19. 简单残局：兵的残局 Basic Endgame: Pawn Endgames**
- Duration: 15 min
- Objectives: Win with a crossed-river pawn supported by the general; understand when a single pawn can or cannot win; coordinate pawn and general movement
- Practice exercises: 6
- Prerequisite: Lesson 18

**20. 综合复习与测验 Comprehensive Review & Quiz**
- Duration: 20 min
- Objectives: Pass a 30-question written quiz covering all material; play a timed game applying all concepts; identify personal weaknesses for Tier 2 study
- Practice exercises: 30 (quiz) + 1 (game)
- Prerequisite: All Tier 1 lessons

## Platform Recommendations

### English-Language Platforms
| Platform | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|----------|------|------|----------|
| **Teachable** | Full control, custom branding, good analytics | Monthly fee ($39-119/mo), must drive own traffic | Primary platform for English audience |
| **Udemy** | Huge marketplace, built-in traffic | Heavy discounting, lower revenue share (37% from organic) | Discovery and volume sales |
| **Skillshare** | Per-minute payment model, good for short courses | Lower per-student revenue, must fit their format | Supplementary income |

### Chinese-Language Platforms
| Platform | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|----------|------|------|----------|
| **网易云课堂** | Large user base for educational content, good tools | Revenue share, competitive market | Primary platform for Chinese audience |
| **腾讯课堂** | Integration with WeChat/QQ ecosystem | Less flexible pricing | QQ/WeChat-sourced students |
| **哔哩哔哩课堂** | Direct integration with Bilibili audience | Newer platform, smaller course market | Converting Bilibili subscribers |

### Recommended Setup
- **Primary (English):** Teachable for full courses, with select content on Udemy for discovery
- **Primary (Chinese):** 网易云课堂 for courses, with free previews on Bilibili
- Host video files on platform (do not self-host)
- Supplement with a Notion or Google Drive resource library for downloadable materials (PGN files, FEN packs, PDF summaries)

## Marketing Strategy: Free Content Funnel

### Funnel Structure

```
YouTube/Bilibili Free Videos (awareness)
    ↓
Email List / QQ Group / Discord (engagement)
    ↓
Free Mini-Course: 5 lessons from Tier 1 (trust building)
    ↓
Paid Tier 1 Purchase (conversion)
    ↓
Upsell to Tier 2, Tier 3, Bundle, Coaching
```

### Specific Tactics

1. **Free YouTube/Bilibili content** — Publish 2 videos per week covering interesting positions, puzzles, and game analysis. End each video with a mention of the course for structured learning.

2. **Free mini-course** — Offer Tier 1 Lessons 1, 2, 3, 8, and 15 as a free email-gated mini-course. This gives enough value to demonstrate quality while leaving the student wanting more.

3. **Seasonal promotions** — Run sales during Chinese New Year, Singles' Day (11/11), and Black Friday. Bundle discount increases to 25% during promotions.

4. **Student testimonials** — After the first cohort completes a tier, collect video testimonials and feature them on the course landing page.

5. **Community integration** — Active QQ group and Discord members get a 10% loyalty discount code. This rewards engagement and drives conversions.

6. **Content upgrades** — In free puzzle videos, mention that the course includes 300+ practice exercises with solutions. In game analysis videos, mention that the course teaches the systematic analysis method.
