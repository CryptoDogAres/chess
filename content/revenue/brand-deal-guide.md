# Douyin Brand Deal Guide for Xiangqi Creators

A complete guide to securing and executing brand deals through 巨量星图 (Huge Star Map), Douyin's official creator marketplace. Covers registration, deal types, pricing, pitching, content integration, contracts, and tax obligations for Chinese chess content creators.

---

## 1. What is 巨量星图 (Juliang Xingtu)

巨量星图 is Douyin's official brand collaboration platform operated by ByteDance. It connects brands looking for influencer marketing with content creators who can deliver their message to a relevant audience. Think of it as a marketplace: brands post campaign briefs, and creators apply or get invited to participate.

### Why it matters for xiangqi creators

- It is the **only legitimate channel** for paid brand integrations on Douyin. Accepting off-platform payments violates Douyin's terms of service and can result in account penalties, shadow-banning, or outright bans.
- Payments are handled through the platform, providing legal protection for both sides.
- It gives you access to a dashboard showing available campaigns, your performance metrics, and your marketplace ranking.
- Brands searching for creators in the 教育 (education) or 传统文化 (traditional culture) verticals will find you through 星图's search and recommendation system.

### How the workflow operates

1. A brand creates a campaign brief on 星图, specifying requirements (video style, talking points, deliverables, timeline).
2. The brand either invites specific creators or opens the campaign for applications.
3. You accept or apply, then negotiate terms if needed.
4. You produce the content, submit it for brand review within 星图.
5. After brand approval, you publish the video on your Douyin account.
6. Payment is released after the video meets the agreed performance window (usually 7-30 days post-publish).

## 2. Registration Requirements

Not every Douyin account qualifies. 巨量星图 has minimum thresholds to ensure brands work with established creators.

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Minimum followers | 10,000 (一万粉丝) |
| Account age | At least 30 days since account creation |
| Real-name verification | 实名认证 — requires a valid Chinese ID card (身份证) |
| Content consistency | Regular posting history (no long gaps of inactivity) |
| Community standing | No active violations or warnings on the account |
| Account type | Must be a 创作者账号 (Creator Account), not a personal account |

**If you are below 10K followers:** Focus on growing your account first. Refer to our Douyin growth guide for strategies. Some MCN agencies (多频道网络) can help you access 星图 at lower follower counts, but they take a revenue share (typically 20-50%), so weigh this carefully.

## 3. How to Register: Step-by-Step

### 星图创作者入驻流程 (Creator Onboarding Process)

**Step 1: Access the platform**
- Open a web browser and go to [star.toutiao.com](https://star.toutiao.com)
- Alternatively, within the Douyin app: 我 → 创作者服务中心 → 任务与活动 → 巨量星图

**Step 2: Log in with your Douyin account**
- Use the same account you publish content on. The platform will automatically pull your account data (followers, engagement rate, content category).

**Step 3: Complete creator profile**
- Fill in your content category: select 教育 (Education) or 文化 (Culture) as your primary vertical.
- Add secondary tags: 象棋, 棋牌, 传统文化, 益智.
- Upload a creator introduction video or select one of your existing Douyin videos as a showcase.
- Write a brief creator description emphasizing your niche and audience demographics.

**Step 4: Set your base pricing**
- The platform will suggest a price range based on your follower count and engagement metrics.
- You can adjust within the allowed range. Start at the suggested midpoint and adjust based on demand.
- You will set prices for different deliverable types: 视频植入 (video integration), 视频定制 (custom video), and 直播推广 (live stream promotion).

**Step 5: Submit for review**
- 星图 reviews your application within 1-3 business days.
- Common rejection reasons: insufficient followers, too few recent posts, account violations.
- You can reapply after addressing the issue.

**Step 6: Confirmation**
- Once approved, your profile appears in the 星图 creator marketplace.
- Brands can now find you, and you can browse and apply to open campaigns.

## 4. Types of Brand Deals for Xiangqi Creators

Your niche determines what brands will approach you. Xiangqi creators sit at the intersection of several advertiser categories.

### Tier 1: High relevance (easy sell, natural fit)

**Chess app promotions (棋类应用推广)**
- Apps like 天天象棋, 弈客象棋, and 微象棋 regularly run user acquisition campaigns.
- Typical ask: show yourself using the app, highlight a specific feature, include a download link.
- These are the most natural brand deals for your content because the product is exactly what your audience uses.
- Expected pricing: ¥3,000-15,000 per video depending on your follower count.

**Online education platforms (在线教育平台)**
- Platforms selling courses (not just chess — general education like 学而思, 猿辅导, or niche platforms offering 象棋课程).
- Typical ask: mention the platform during a lesson-style video, position it as a learning resource.
- Your audience skews toward learners, making this a strong category match.

### Tier 2: Good relevance (requires slight creative adaptation)

**Gaming peripherals and tech (游戏外设与数码)**
- Monitors, tablets, phone stands, ring lights, mice, and keyboards.
- Angle: "This is what I use to create my content" or "This screen makes analyzing board positions easier."
- Brands in this space include: 雷蛇 (Razer), 罗技 (Logitech), various domestic brands.
- These brands have large budgets and work across many creator verticals.

**Traditional culture brands (传统文化品牌)**
- Tea brands (茶叶): xiangqi and tea culture have deep historical overlap. "Let me sip some [brand] tea while we analyze this classic game."
- Stationery and calligraphy supplies (文房四宝): brushes, ink, paper, ink stones — appeals to the traditional aesthetic your audience appreciates.
- Board game sets and premium chess pieces: physical xiangqi sets, decorative boards.
- Traditional Chinese medicine (中医养生) brands targeting the older demographic common in xiangqi audiences.

### Tier 3: Tangential (requires larger audience)

**General lifestyle brands (大众消费品)**
- Food and beverage, clothing, household products.
- These brands care less about niche relevance and more about raw audience size.
- Typically only available to creators with 500K+ followers.
- You will need to be creative about integration: "I always have [brand snack] next to me during long analysis sessions."

## 5. Pricing Your Content

### The pricing formula

A reasonable starting point for calculating your rate:

```
Base price = (Average views per video ÷ 1,000) × CPM rate × content premium
```

Where:
- **CPM rate** for Douyin creator content ranges from ¥20-80 depending on niche (education/culture niches command higher CPMs than entertainment).
- **Content premium** is a multiplier for your engagement rate and audience quality:
  - Engagement rate above 5%: multiply by 1.3-1.5
  - Highly loyal audience (high comment-to-view ratio): multiply by 1.2
  - Niche authority (recognized as an expert): multiply by 1.2-1.5

### Benchmarks by follower tier

| Follower Count | Average Views | Typical Price per Video | Notes |
|---------------|---------------|------------------------|-------|
| 10K-50K | 5K-20K | ¥1,000-5,000 | Entry-level; accept deals for portfolio building |
| 50K-100K | 20K-80K | ¥3,000-12,000 | Steady deal flow possible |
| 100K-500K | 50K-300K | ¥5,000-50,000 | Strong earning range; most brand campaigns target this tier |
| 500K-1M | 200K-800K | ¥30,000-80,000 | Premium rates; brands compete for your slots |
| 1M-5M | 500K-2M | ¥50,000-120,000 | Top-tier creator; can be selective |
| 5M+ | 1M+ | ¥100,000-200,000+ | Elite level |

**Real benchmark:** 王铁锤, a top xiangqi creator with approximately 14 million followers, commands an estimated ¥148,000-178,000 per sponsored video. This represents the upper ceiling for the niche. Use this as a reference point but understand it reflects years of audience building and a massive follower base.

### What affects your rate

- **Exclusivity:** If a brand wants you to avoid competitors for 30-90 days, charge 1.5-2x your standard rate.
- **Usage rights:** If the brand wants to repurpose your video as an ad (投放素材), charge an additional 50-100% on top.
- **Revision rounds:** One round of revisions is standard. Each additional round should add 10-20% to the fee.
- **Turnaround time:** Rush orders (under 3 days) warrant a 30-50% premium.

## 7. Content Integration Best Practices

The difference between a brand deal that earns you repeat business and one that damages your reputation is how naturally you weave the promotion into your content.

### The golden rule

**If a viewer could remove the sponsored segment and the video would feel incomplete, you have integrated it correctly.** If the sponsored segment feels like an interruption, you have failed.

### Techniques for natural integration

**Technique 1: The tool-in-action approach**
- Instead of stopping your content to talk about the product, use it as part of your workflow.
- Example for a chess app: "Let me pull up this position in [app name] to show you the engine evaluation" — then naturally continue your analysis using the app's interface.
- Example for a monitor: film your usual analysis session but mention, "I recently switched to this [brand] monitor and the larger screen makes it much easier to show you these complex positions."

**Technique 2: The opening hook integration**
- Place the sponsorship mention in the first 10 seconds alongside your content hook.
- "Today's puzzle is sponsored by [brand] — and speaking of puzzles, this endgame position stumped a grandmaster for 20 minutes."

**Technique 3: The dedicated review (for highly relevant products)**
- For chess apps or physical chess sets, a dedicated review video is entirely natural.
- Your audience actually wants to know which apps and tools you recommend.
- Frame it as a genuine review, including both strengths and limitations. Audiences detect dishonesty instantly.

### Legal disclosure requirements

Chinese advertising law (广告法) and Douyin's platform rules require clear disclosure of sponsored content.

- 星图 automatically adds a "广告" (advertisement) label to videos published through the platform.
- In your video, verbally mention the sponsorship: "本期视频由[品牌]赞助" (This video is sponsored by [brand]) or "感谢[品牌]对本期内容的支持" (Thanks to [brand] for supporting this content).
- Do not hide or obscure the sponsorship label in your video editing.
- Failure to disclose can result in fines under the advertising law and penalties from Douyin.

### What to avoid

- **Do not sacrifice chess content quality.** If a brand wants you to spend 60 seconds talking about their product in a 90-second video, decline or negotiate.
- **Do not accept deals for products you would never use.** Your audience trusts you because of your chess expertise; promoting irrelevant or low-quality products erodes that trust quickly.
- **Do not make false claims.** If a chess app has bugs, do not claim it is perfect. Honest enthusiasm is more persuasive than forced praise.
- **Do not accept competing deals simultaneously.** If you are promoting one chess app, do not promote its direct competitor the following week. This destroys credibility with both brands and your audience.

## 8. Contract Essentials: Key Terms to Negotiate

Whether the deal is arranged through 星图's standard flow or involves a separate agreement, understand these terms before accepting.

### Terms to always confirm in writing

| Term | What to look for |
|------|-----------------|
| 合作费用 (Fee) | Total amount, currency (RMB), whether it includes tax or is pre-tax |
| 付款时间 (Payment timeline) | When payment is released — upon video approval, upon publication, or after a performance window |
| 内容要求 (Content requirements) | Exact deliverables: number of videos, minimum duration, required talking points, visual assets to include |
| 修改次数 (Revision rounds) | How many rounds of brand feedback are included (standard: 1-2 rounds; push for a cap) |
| 发布时间 (Publication deadline) | When the video must go live; negotiate enough production time |
| 数据保证 (Performance guarantee) | Whether the brand requires minimum views; avoid guaranteeing specific view counts — you cannot control the algorithm |
| 排他期 (Exclusivity period) | How long you must avoid promoting competitors; shorter is better for you (aim for 15-30 days max) |
| 素材授权 (Content licensing) | Whether the brand can reuse your video as paid advertising; if yes, charge extra and set a time limit |
| 违约条款 (Breach terms) | Penalties for late delivery, missed targets, or early video deletion; ensure penalties are proportional |

### Red flags in contracts

- **Unlimited revision rounds.** Brands will sometimes iterate endlessly. Cap it at 2 rounds with additional rounds billed hourly.
- **Performance-only payment.** If the brand only pays based on views or conversions, you bear all the risk. Insist on a base fee plus any performance bonus.
- **Permanent content licensing.** Your video should not be used as brand advertising forever. Set a 6-12 month license window.
- **Vague deliverable descriptions.** "Create a video about our product" is too vague. Insist on specific shot lists, required mentions, and duration ranges.

## 9. Tax Implications: 个人所得税 for Creator Income

Brand deal income is taxable in China. Understanding your obligations prevents surprises.

### How 星图 handles taxes

- For deals processed through 星图, ByteDance acts as a withholding agent in most cases.
- The platform deducts 个人所得税 (individual income tax) before paying you.
- The applicable tax category is 劳务报酬所得 (income from personal services).

### Tax rates for 劳务报酬所得

| Taxable Income per Transaction | Tax Rate | Quick Deduction |
|-------------------------------|----------|-----------------|
| Up to ¥20,000 | 20% | ¥0 |
| ¥20,001 - ¥50,000 | 30% | ¥2,000 |
| Over ¥50,000 | 40% | ¥7,000 |

**Note:** Before applying the tax rate, a deduction is applied to the gross income:
- Income ≤ ¥4,000: deduct ¥800
- Income > ¥4,000: deduct 20% of the gross amount

### Example calculation

You receive a brand deal payment of ¥30,000:
1. Deduction: ¥30,000 × 20% = ¥6,000
2. Taxable income: ¥30,000 - ¥6,000 = ¥24,000
3. Tax rate: 30% (falls in the ¥20,001-50,000 bracket)
4. Tax: ¥24,000 × 30% - ¥2,000 = ¥5,200
5. Net payment: ¥30,000 - ¥5,200 = ¥24,800

### Reducing your tax burden legally

- **Register as a 个体工商户 (Individual business).** This changes your tax category and can reduce your effective rate, especially at higher income levels. Consult an accountant for your specific situation.
- **Establish a studio or company (工作室/公司).** At significant income levels (¥500K+ annually from brand deals), operating through a business entity provides more tax optimization options.
- **Keep records of all business expenses.** Equipment purchases, software subscriptions, internet costs, and studio rent can potentially offset taxable income if you operate as a registered business.
- **File annual tax reconciliation (年度汇算清缴).** Even if taxes are withheld by 星图, you must participate in the annual tax reconciliation (typically March-June) to ensure correct final tax amounts. Overpayments get refunded; underpayments must be settled.

### Record keeping

Maintain organized records of:
- All 星图 transaction receipts and payment confirmations
- Contracts for each brand deal
- Invoices (发票) issued or received
- Business-related expenses with receipts
- Annual income summaries from 星图's creator dashboard
