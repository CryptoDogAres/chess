# Setting Up Live Stream Monetization

A platform-by-platform guide for enabling tips, gifts, and subscriptions on Douyin, Bilibili, and YouTube live streams. Includes requirements, revenue splits, setup steps, and strategies for encouraging viewer support naturally.

---

## Overview: Live Stream Revenue Potential

Live streaming is one of the fastest paths to direct monetization for content creators. Unlike ad revenue (which requires large view counts) or sponsorships (which require brand deals), live stream tips and gifts can start generating income as soon as you meet basic platform requirements.

For xiangqi content, live streams are particularly effective because:
- Real-time puzzle solving creates interactive moments where viewers feel involved
- Game analysis feels like a shared experience, building emotional connection
- The "teacher-student" dynamic natural to chess content encourages viewer appreciation
- Xiangqi has a dedicated community of enthusiasts willing to support creators they value

This guide covers the three platforms in order of likely first revenue: Douyin (lowest barrier), Bilibili (mid barrier), YouTube (highest barrier but highest per-viewer value).

## 1. Douyin (抖音) Live Stream Monetization

### Requirements to Enable Live Gifts

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Minimum followers | 1,000 |
| Real-name verification | 实名认证 — requires Chinese ID card |
| Account age | At least 30 days |
| No community guideline violations | Clean account standing |
| Content category | Must not be in restricted categories (chess/xiangqi is fine) |

### How to Enable

1. Open Douyin app → Profile → Menu (三) → 创作者服务中心 (Creator Service Center)
2. Navigate to 直播 (Live Stream) section
3. Apply for 直播权限 (Live streaming permission) if not already enabled
4. Once approved, gifts are automatically enabled for your live streams
5. Set up 抖音钱包 (Douyin Wallet) to receive earnings: Profile → Wallet → Bind bank card

### Gift Types and Values

Douyin viewers purchase "抖币" (Douyin coins) with real money, then spend coins on virtual gifts during your stream. Each gift has a coin value, and you receive a percentage of the coin value as revenue.

| Gift Name | Icon | Coin Cost | Approx RMB Value | Frequency |
|-----------|------|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| 小心心 (Heart) | Heart | 1 coin | ¥0.1 | Very common, low value |
| 棒棒糖 (Lollipop) | Lollipop | 9 coins | ¥0.9 | Common |
| 玫瑰 (Rose) | Rose | 10 coins | ¥1 | Common |
| 啤酒 (Beer) | Beer | 19 coins | ¥1.9 | Moderate |
| 仙女棒 (Fairy Wand) | Wand | 199 coins | ¥19.9 | Occasional |
| 浪漫烟花 (Fireworks) | Fireworks | 1,000 coins | ¥100 | Rare, high value |
| 嘉年华 (Carnival) | Carnival | 3,000 coins | ¥300 | Very rare, very high value |

**Note**: Gift types and values change periodically. Check the current gift panel in the Douyin app for the latest options.

### Revenue Split

**Creator receives approximately 50% of the gift value.**

The exact split depends on your level and activity:
- Base rate: 50% for most creators
- Top creators with MCN agencies may negotiate higher rates (up to 60-70%)
- Douyin takes the remaining 50% as platform fee
- Earnings are calculated daily and visible in your 直播收益 (Live Revenue) dashboard
- Minimum withdrawal: ¥5
- Withdrawal method: Bank transfer, typically arrives within 1-3 business days

### How to Encourage Tips Naturally During Xiangqi Streams

The key word is "naturally." Aggressive begging for gifts will drive viewers away. Instead, create moments where viewers feel genuinely motivated to show appreciation.

**Strategy 1: The Puzzle Thank-You Loop**
- Solve a puzzle live and explain it brilliantly
- When someone sends a gift, immediately acknowledge them by name: "谢谢[用户名]的棒棒糖！看来你也找到正确答案了" (Thanks [username] for the lollipop! Looks like you found the right answer too)
- This public acknowledgment motivates others to gift because they want to be recognized

**Strategy 2: Gift-Triggered Content**
- Set up an on-stream rule: "Every time someone sends a rose, I'll solve a new puzzle" or "Send a beer and I'll analyze one of your games"
- This gives viewers a reason to gift beyond just tipping — they are purchasing content
- Keep the threshold low (affordable gifts) to encourage participation

**Strategy 3: Milestone Celebrations**
- Set viewer-visible goals: "If we reach 100 gifts this stream, I'll do a special speed-solve challenge"
- The collective goal motivates the group, not just individuals
- Celebrate when milestones are hit with genuine enthusiasm

**Strategy 4: The PK Format**
- Douyin has a built-in PK (player-killer) feature where two streamers compete live
- During a PK, viewers gift to support their streamer. This naturally drives gift volume.
- PK against other chess or board game streamers for cross-audience exposure

**What NOT to do**:
- Do not say "请送礼物" (please send gifts) repeatedly
- Do not make viewers feel guilty for watching without gifting
- Do not pause content to wait for gifts
- Do not set unreasonably high gift thresholds for content unlocks

## 2. Bilibili (B站) Live Stream Monetization

Bilibili has a more complex monetization system with multiple revenue streams available during live streams.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Minimum followers | 1,000 (for most features) |
| Real-name verification | Required for all monetization |
| Account level | Level 2+ (earned through platform activity) |
| Live streaming permission | Apply through 直播中心 (Live Center) |
| No violations | Clean community standing |

### Monetization Type 1: 充电 (Charge)

充电 is Bilibili's tipping system. Viewers "charge" your content by paying real money.

**How it works**:
- Viewers click the 充电 button on your video or live stream
- They choose an amount (starting from 2 B币, approximately ¥2)
- Your content gets a "充电" badge showing how many people charged it

**Revenue split**: Creator receives **70%** of charge revenue. Bilibili takes 30%.

**How to enable**:
1. Go to Bilibili Creator Center (创作中心)
2. Navigate to 收益管理 (Revenue Management)
3. Enable 充电计划 (Charge Plan)
4. Bind your payment method

### Monetization Type 2: 舰长/提督/总督 (Captain/Admiral/Governor)

This is Bilibili's tiered membership subscription system, specific to live streaming. Viewers pay monthly to become a "船员" (crew member) at different ranks.

| Tier | Chinese Name | Monthly Cost | What Viewers Get |
|------|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| 舰长 (Captain) | 舰长 | ¥138/month | Special chat badge, exclusive emotes, entry announcement |
| 提督 (Admiral) | 提督 | ¥1,998/month | Premium badge, louder entry announcement, priority in chat |
| 总督 (Governor) | 总督 | ¥19,998/month | Top-tier badge, server-wide entry announcement, VIP treatment |

**Revenue split**: Creator receives approximately **70%** of subscription revenue. The exact rate may vary based on your agreement with Bilibili or your MCN.

**Reality check**: For a xiangqi channel, most crew members will be 舰长 (Captain) level. 提督 and 总督 are extremely rare and typically seen only on top entertainment streamers. Do not build your revenue model expecting high-tier subscriptions.

**How to enable**:
1. Must have live streaming enabled
2. Go to 直播中心 → 舰长系统
3. Follow the setup wizard
4. Set custom perks for each tier (see below)

**Recommended perks for xiangqi channel**:
- 舰长 (Captain): Exclusive endgame puzzle set each month (PDF or image), special badge, name appears on stream overlay, monthly private lesson (15 minutes, if feasible)
- 提督 (Admiral): All captain perks + personalized game analysis, name in video credits
- 总督 (Governor): All perks + anything they want (at this price, provide premium service)

### Monetization Type 3: Live Stream Gifts (礼物)

Similar to Douyin, Bilibili has virtual gifts that viewers can send during live streams.

| Gift | Cost (金瓜子/Gold Melon Seeds) | Approx RMB |
|------|-------------------------------|------------|
| 辣条 (Spicy Strip) | 100 seeds | ¥0.1 |
| 小电视 (Small TV) | 500 seeds | ¥0.5 |
| B坷垃 | 9,900 seeds | ¥9.9 |
| 摩天大楼 (Skyscraper) | 20,000 seeds | ¥20 |
| 小电视飞船 (TV Spaceship) | 1,245,000 seeds | ¥1,245 |

**Revenue split**: Creator receives **50-70%** depending on the gift type and your creator level.

### Bilibili-Specific Strategies for Xiangqi Streams

**Strategy 1: 弹幕互动 (Danmaku Interaction)**
- Bilibili's signature feature is real-time bullet comments (弹幕)
- During live puzzle solving, ask viewers to type their answer in danmaku
- This creates a visible flood of engagement that encourages more participation
- Active danmaku attracts casual browsers who see the lively chat

**Strategy 2: The 三连 (Triple Combo) Ask**
- On Bilibili, the highest compliment is a "三连" — like + coin + favorite
- At the end of a particularly good analysis, say: "如果觉得有帮助，一键三连支持一下" (If you found this helpful, hit the triple combo to show support)
- This is a culturally accepted and expected ask on Bilibili

**Strategy 3: 舰长 Exclusives**
- Run occasional "captains only" segments during your stream where you analyze subscriber-submitted games
- This gives viewers a tangible reason to subscribe at the 舰长 level
- Keep it to 10-15 minutes of a 1-2 hour stream. Do not make free viewers feel excluded.

**Strategy 4: PK and Collaboration**
- Bilibili supports live PK between streamers
- Collaborate with other chess/board game streamers
- Cross-pollinate audiences for mutual growth

## 3. YouTube Live Stream Monetization

YouTube has the highest barriers to entry but offers the most lucrative per-viewer monetization for English-speaking or international audiences.

### Requirements

| Feature | Requirement |
|---------|------------|
| Live streaming | Phone-verified account, no live stream restrictions in the past 90 days |
| Super Chat | YouTube Partner Program: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (or 10M Shorts views) |
| Super Stickers | Same as Super Chat |
| Channel Memberships | 1,000 subscribers (previously 30K, threshold lowered) |

### Monetization Type 1: Super Chat

Super Chat allows viewers to pay to pin their message in the live chat. The message appears highlighted and stays pinned for a duration based on the payment amount.

| Amount (USD) | Pin Duration | Chat Color |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $1-1.99 | 0 seconds (highlighted only) | Blue |
| $2-4.99 | 30 seconds | Cyan |
| $5-9.99 | 1 minute | Green |
| $10-19.99 | 2 minutes | Yellow |
| $20-49.99 | 5 minutes | Orange |
| $50-99.99 | 10 minutes | Magenta |
| $100-500 | 1 hour | Red |

**Revenue split**: Creator receives **70%**. YouTube takes 30%.

**How to enable**:
1. Join the YouTube Partner Program (apply through YouTube Studio → Monetization)
2. Once accepted, go to YouTube Studio → Monetization → Supers
3. Enable Super Chat and Super Stickers
4. During any live stream, Super Chat is automatically available to viewers

### Monetization Type 2: Channel Memberships

Channel memberships are monthly subscriptions where viewers pay to become a "member" of your channel. You set the tiers and perks.

**Default pricing**: $4.99/month (you can add additional tiers at higher prices)

**Recommended tier structure for xiangqi channel**:

| Tier | Price | Name Suggestion | Perks |
|------|-------|----------------|-------|
| Tier 1 | $4.99/month | 棋友 (Chess Friend) | Custom badge, 4 custom emojis, members-only posts |
| Tier 2 | $9.99/month | 棋士 (Chess Scholar) | All Tier 1 perks + early access to videos (24h before public), members-only live streams (monthly) |
| Tier 3 | $24.99/month | 棋圣 (Chess Master) | All Tier 2 perks + personal game analysis (1 per month), name in video credits |

**Revenue split**: Creator receives **70%**. YouTube takes 30%.

**Custom emojis to create**:
- A xiangqi piece (将/帅)
- A thinking face with a chess board
- A "brilliant move" indicator
- A "blunder" indicator

These emojis can be used by members in live chat, creating visible social proof that encourages others to join.

**How to enable**:
1. YouTube Studio → Monetization → Memberships
2. Set up tiers and pricing
3. Upload custom badges (for 1-month, 2-month, 6-month, 12-month, 24-month members)
4. Upload custom emojis
5. Create a welcome post for new members

### YouTube-Specific Strategies for Xiangqi Streams

**Strategy 1: Super Chat Puzzle Queue**
- During live streams, tell viewers: "Send a Super Chat with a position, and I'll analyze it live"
- This gives Super Chat a clear value proposition beyond just tipping
- Prioritize Super Chat questions over free chat questions (this is expected and fair)
- Even $2 Super Chats add up over a 2-hour stream

**Strategy 2: Members-Only Streams**
- Run one members-only live stream per month
- Content: exclusive game analysis, Q&A, or interactive puzzle sessions
- This gives members a tangible reason to maintain their subscription
- Announce the schedule in advance so members can plan to attend

**Strategy 3: Read Every Super Chat**
- In smaller streams (under 100 concurrent viewers, which is likely your starting point), read and respond to every single Super Chat
- This personal attention is what keeps viewers sending Super Chats
- Use the viewer's name: "Great question from [Name]! Let me show you..."
- If you get too many Super Chats to read during the stream, do a "Super Chat catch-up" segment at the end

**Strategy 4: Membership Milestone Goals**
- Share your membership goals openly: "We're at 45 members. When we reach 50, I'll do a 3-hour marathon analysis stream"
- Community goals create a sense of shared progress
- Celebrate milestones publicly and thank your members

**Strategy 5: End-of-Stream Gratitude**
- In the last 5 minutes of every stream, scroll through and thank all Super Chat donors and new members by name
- This is your most important monetization habit. Viewers who feel appreciated will return and give again.

## Getting Started: First Live Stream Checklist

Before your first monetized live stream, complete these preparations.

### Technical Setup
- [ ] Stable internet connection (upload speed >5 Mbps, wired connection preferred)
- [ ] Streaming software configured (OBS Studio recommended, or use in-app streaming)
- [ ] Camera and microphone tested (audio quality matters more than video quality)
- [ ] Chess board clearly visible in the stream (digital board or physical board with overhead camera)
- [ ] Stream overlay set up: your logo, social handles, "follow/subscribe" reminder

### Monetization Setup
- [ ] Douyin: Wallet configured, bank card bound, gift system enabled
- [ ] Bilibili: 充电 enabled, 舰长 system set up with custom perks, gift system active
- [ ] YouTube: YPP accepted (if qualified), Super Chat enabled, memberships configured with tiers and emojis

### Content Preparation
- [ ] 3-5 puzzles prepared for live solving (in case of slow chat moments)
- [ ] One game prepared for analysis (your "main content" for the stream)
- [ ] Interactive segment planned: viewer-submitted positions or vote-for-the-next-move
- [ ] Stream title and description written with relevant keywords

### During the Stream
- [ ] Greet viewers by name as they join
- [ ] Acknowledge every gift and Super Chat within 30 seconds
- [ ] Mix content types: analysis, puzzles, viewer interaction
- [ ] Mention your schedule: "I stream every [day] at [time]"
- [ ] End with a thank-you segment for all donors and new followers/subscribers

### After the Stream
- [ ] Check revenue dashboard on each platform
- [ ] Note which moments generated the most gifts (replicate these)
- [ ] Export highlight clips for Douyin/Shorts (see highlight-clips.md)
- [ ] Post a thank-you message to your community
- [ ] Update your engagement tracking spreadsheet
