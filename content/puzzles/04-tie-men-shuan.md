# 铁门栓杀法 — Iron Door Bolt

## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

"古人说铁门栓，栓上了，门就再也打不开了。今天这盘棋，黑方的九宫就是那扇被锁死的门！"
(The ancients called it the Iron Door Bolt — once bolted, the door never opens again. Today, Black's palace is that locked door!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"局面很简单。红方一匹马在中间偏左的位置，一门炮在下面中路。黑方呢？将在中间，士象齐全，看着挺稳的。"
(The position is simple. Red has a knight in the center-left, a cannon below on the center file. Black? General in the middle, advisors and elephants all present — looks solid.)

"但是我告诉你，红方两步之内就能锁死黑方的大门。铁门栓的关键在哪里？在马和炮的双重控制——马卡位，炮封门！"
(But I'm telling you, Red can lock Black's door in two moves. Where's the key to the Iron Door Bolt? The dual control of knight and cannon — knight blocks the position, cannon seals the door!)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

"红方先走哪一步？是先动马还是先动炮？暂停视频想一想！三、二、一——"
(Which piece does Red move first? Knight or cannon? Pause and think! Three, two, one—)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:50)

"很多人的第一反应——炮先冲上去！炮五进六，直接打到黑方底线，将军！痛快！但是等一下……黑方将往旁边走一步，躲开了炮的攻击线。然后你的马还没到位，炮一个人孤军深入，黑方出车一反击，你的炮就危险了。"
(Many people's first instinct — cannon charges up first! Cannon advances to Black's back rank, check! Satisfying! But wait... Black's general steps to the side, dodging the cannon's attack line. Your knight isn't in position yet, the cannon is deep in enemy territory alone, and Black's chariot counterattacks — your cannon is in danger.)

"单打独斗不行，得两个人一起上！"
(Going solo won't work — you need both pieces working together!)

## Correct Move Reveal (0:50-1:40)

"正确走法——马先跳！马从当前位置跳到黑方九宫的角上。这一步非常关键，为什么？因为马到了这个位置之后，做了三件事情："
(Correct move — knight jumps first! Knight moves to the corner of Black's palace. This move is critical. Why? Because from this position, the knight does three things:)

"第一，控制住了将的两个逃跑格子。第二，给炮创造了进攻的条件。第三，马本身也在威胁将！"
(First, it controls two of the general's escape squares. Second, it creates attacking conditions for the cannon. Third, the knight itself is threatening the general!)

"黑方怎么应对？如果走将，将四进一——红方炮五进六！炮沿着中路直接打上去，将军！这时候黑将想跑回来？不行，马在旁边卡着呢。想往上走？炮控制着中线。"
(How does Black respond? If the general moves forward — Red plays cannon advance! Cannon fires straight up the center file, check! Now the general wants to come back? Can't — the knight is blocking right there. Go up? The cannon controls the center line.)

"黑将上下左右全被封死了！马卡左右，炮封上下，这就像一把大铁栓，咔嚓一声，门锁死了！绝杀！"
(The Black general is sealed in every direction! Knight blocks left and right, cannon seals top and bottom — just like a giant iron bolt, click, the door is locked! Checkmate!)

"这就是铁门栓——马和炮的完美配合，把对方的将锁在九宫里出不来！"
(This is the Iron Door Bolt — perfect coordination between knight and cannon, locking the opponent's general inside the palace with no way out!)

## Principle (1:40-1:55)

"铁门栓的核心原则是：进攻要有节奏。不是谁先冲上去谁厉害，而是要按顺序来——先卡位再进攻。马先到位堵住逃路，炮再来收人头。记住这四个字：先堵后杀。"
(The core principle of the Iron Door Bolt: attack with rhythm. It's not about who charges first — it's about sequence. Position first, then attack. Knight gets in place to block escape, then cannon comes for the kill. Remember these four characters: block first, kill second.)
