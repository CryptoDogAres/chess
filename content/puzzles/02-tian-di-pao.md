## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

"两门炮，一个天上一个地下，黑方的将直接动弹不得——这就是天地炮！"
(Two cannons, one above one below — the Black general can't move an inch — this is the Heaven & Earth Cannons!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"看这个局面：红方有两门炮。一门炮已经沉到了黑方的底线，我们叫它'地炮'，踩在人家家门口。另一门炮在中路上方，这是'天炮'。黑方的将在中间，两个士两个象守着。看起来黑方的防守很严密，是不是？"
(Look at this position: Red has two cannons. One cannon has already sunk to Black's back rank — we call it the 'Earth Cannon,' right on the doorstep. The other is on the upper center file — the 'Heaven Cannon.' Black's general is in the middle, guarded by two advisors and two elephants. Black's defense looks tight, doesn't it?)

"但是红方只需要两步，就能让黑方的城池土崩瓦解！"
(But Red only needs two moves to make Black's fortress crumble!)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:50)

"有人说：天炮直接冲下去！炮从上面一路杀到底线，将军！听起来很爽对吧？但问题是——你两门炮挤在一条线上了，互相挡着。黑方士一支，轻松化解。你的两门炮反而变成了摆设。"
(Some say: charge the Heaven Cannon straight down! Cannon rushes to the back rank — check! Sounds great, right? But the problem is — your two cannons are now crowded on the same line, blocking each other. Black plays one advisor move, easily resolved. Your two cannons become decorations.)

"两门炮在一条线上，那叫'重炮'，不叫'天地炮'。天地炮的精髓是什么？错开！"
(Two cannons on one line — that's 'stacked cannons,' not 'Heaven & Earth Cannons.' The essence of Heaven & Earth is what? Offset positioning!)

## Correct Move Reveal (0:50-1:35)

"正确的走法：天炮不要急着往下沉！天炮先平移，走到中路正上方。为什么？因为天炮要从上面控制住将的头顶，地炮从下面封住退路。"
(The correct move: don't rush the Heaven Cannon down! The Heaven Cannon shifts laterally first, moving above the center. Why? Because the Heaven Cannon controls the general from above, while the Earth Cannon seals the retreat from below.)

"黑方怎么办？出士？出士就给地炮让开了炮架，地炮直接将军。不出士？那天炮从上面压下来，两面夹击！"
(What can Black do? Move an advisor? That creates a cannon mount for the Earth Cannon — instant check. Don't move the advisor? Then the Heaven Cannon presses down from above — pincer attack!)

"黑方走士四进五——天炮平中路将军！黑将只能往旁边躲，将四进一。这时候地炮跟过来——炮平到将的那一路，将军！上面天炮盖着，下面地炮顶着，黑将上天无路入地无门，绝杀！"
(Black plays advisor up — Heaven Cannon slides to center file, check! The general can only dodge sideways. Now the Earth Cannon follows — cannon shifts to the general's file, check! Heaven Cannon covering from above, Earth Cannon pressing from below — the general has no way to heaven, no door to earth — checkmate!)

"漂亮！这就是天地炮——一天一地，绝杀无情！"
(Beautiful! This is the Heaven & Earth Cannons — one in heaven, one on earth, ruthless checkmate!)

## Principle (1:35-1:50)

"天地炮教给我们：空间控制比直接进攻更重要。两门炮不要挤在一起，要一上一下，形成立体的控制网。下棋的时候记住——你的棋子要占据不同的空间，才能发挥最大威力。"
(The Heaven & Earth Cannons teach us: spatial control matters more than direct attack. Don't crowd two cannons together — position them top and bottom to form a 3D control net. Remember when playing — your pieces need to occupy different spaces to maximize their power.)
