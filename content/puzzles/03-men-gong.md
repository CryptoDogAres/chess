# 闷宫杀 — Smothered Checkmate

- **Duration**: 2:10 estimated
- **Platform**: Douyin (vertical) + YouTube Shorts

- **Difficulty**: ★★★☆☆

## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

"你见过被自己人活活闷死的将吗？今天这盘棋，黑方的将就是这么死的——太惨了！"
(Have you ever seen a general suffocated to death by his own pieces? Today's puzzle — that's exactly how Black's general dies. Brutal!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"来看局面。红方就一个车，在左边。黑方呢？将在中间，两个士两个象全在，还有一个马一个车。从子力上看，黑方应该更强才对。但是——黑方有个致命的问题。"
(Look at the position. Red has just one chariot, on the left. Black? General in the center, both advisors and both elephants present, plus a knight and a chariot. Material-wise, Black should be stronger. But — Black has a fatal problem.)

"看到了吗？黑方的将被自己的士、象围得严严实实的，一步都动不了。这就是闷宫的前提——对方的将被自己的棋子堵住了！"
(See it? Black's general is completely hemmed in by his own advisors and elephants — can't move a single step. That's the prerequisite for a Smothered Checkmate — the opponent's general is blocked by his own pieces!)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

"红方只有一个车，怎么利用黑方自己的棋子来将死黑将？暂停视频想一想！三、二、一——"
(Red has only one chariot — how do you use Black's own pieces to checkmate? Pause and think! Three, two, one—)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:50)

"第一个错误思路：车直接杀到底线！车一平四，将军！看起来很凶对不对？但是黑方的车可以过来挡啊！黑车往中路一摆，挡住了红车的将军，然后黑方的马还能跳过来反攻。红方一个车对黑方一车一马，根本打不过。"
(First wrong idea: chariot straight to the back rank! Chariot slides to the 4th file, check! Looks fierce right? But Black's chariot can come block! Black chariot moves to the center, blocks the check, then Black's knight can jump over to counterattack. One Red chariot versus Black's chariot and knight — no contest.)

## Bad Move #2 (0:50-1:10)

"第二个错误：从侧面进攻，车走到右边去。想法是绕过去从另一边杀。但问题是——你给了黑方时间！黑方趁这个机会，走一步士退下来，将的活动空间一下子就打开了。闷宫的条件没了，你再怎么攻也攻不进去了。"
(Second mistake: attack from the side, chariot goes to the right. The idea is to circle around. But the problem — you're giving Black time! Black uses this chance to pull an advisor back, and suddenly the general has breathing room. The smothered condition is gone — you'll never break through now.)

"闷宫杀最怕什么？最怕对方有时间拆开防守！"
(What's the Smothered Checkmate's biggest enemy? Giving the opponent time to open up the defense!)

## Correct Move Reveal (1:10-1:45)

"正确走法：车直接冲到黑方底线的将头上！车二进八，一步到位！注意——不是从侧面绕，是从正面硬冲进去！"
(Correct move: chariot charges straight to Black's back rank, right above the general! Chariot advances eight ranks — one move, done! Note — not circling from the side, but charging straight from the front!)

"黑方能挡吗？黑车想过来挡——但是来不及了！因为红车已经站在将头上了。黑将想跑？往左跑，士挡着。往右跑，象挡着。往上走？红车就在头上！"
(Can Black block? Black chariot wants to come — but too late! Red's chariot is already on top of the general. General wants to run? Left — blocked by advisor. Right — blocked by elephant. Up — Red chariot is right there!)

"黑将被自己人围死了，红车往下一沉——将！闷宫杀成立！黑将哪儿都去不了，棋局结束！"
(The general is walled in by his own pieces. Red chariot drops down — check! Smothered Checkmate complete! The general has nowhere to go — game over!)

"太漂亮了！黑方七个子在场上，愣是一个都帮不上忙，反而全成了帮凶！"
(Beautiful! Black has seven pieces on the board, and not one can help — they're all accomplices!)

## Principle (1:45-2:05)

"闷宫杀教给我们两个道理。第一：防守的时候，不要把自己的将围得太死，要留出呼吸的空间。第二：进攻的时候，要看对方的弱点——如果对方的将动不了，那就是天赐良机，不要犹豫，直接冲进去！"
(Smothered Checkmate teaches us two things. First: when defending, don't wall your general in too tightly — leave breathing room. Second: when attacking, look for weaknesses — if the opponent's general can't move, that's a gift from heaven. Don't hesitate — charge right in!)
