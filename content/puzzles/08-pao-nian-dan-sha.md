## Position Setup (0:05-0:25)

"来看局面。红方有什么？一门炮在左上方，一匹马在中路，还有一个车在最右下角。黑方呢？将在中间，士象马都有，防守很全面。"
(Look at the position. What does Red have? A cannon on the upper left, a knight in the center, and a chariot in the far bottom right. Black? General in the middle, advisors, elephants, and a knight — comprehensive defense.)

"注意看红方这三个子的位置——炮在远处，马在中间，车在角落。看起来红方的子力很分散，根本攻不进去。但是——古人的智慧就在这里：不是攻不进去，是你没找到正确的进攻顺序！"
(Notice the position of Red's three pieces — cannon far away, knight in the middle, chariot in the corner. Looks like Red's forces are scattered, no way to break through. But — here lies the ancients' wisdom: it's not that you can't break through, you just haven't found the right attack sequence!)

## Bad Move #1 (0:35-0:55)

"第一个错误想法：车先冲上去！车从右下角一路杀到黑方底线。看起来很有威胁？但黑方的象和马配合着一防，车被堵在角落里进退不得。而且你的车跑上去了，炮和马还在远处，形不成配合。"
(First wrong idea: chariot charges up first! Chariot rushes from the bottom right all the way to Black's back rank. Looks threatening? But Black's elephant and knight coordinate to defend — the chariot gets stuck in the corner. And your chariot went up alone, while the cannon and knight are still far away — no coordination.)

## Bad Move #2 (0:55-1:15)

"第二个错误：马先跳到黑方九宫附近。马到了将的身边，确实很危险。但是黑方的马可以过来对踢！黑马蹩住你的马腿，你的马反而被牵制住了。进攻变成了纠缠，红方的优势慢慢消失。"
(Second mistake: knight jumps near Black's palace first. Knight near the general is indeed dangerous. But Black's knight can come contest! Black's knight hobbles your knight's legs, and your knight gets pinned down. Attack becomes entanglement, Red's advantage slowly vanishes.)

## Correct Move Reveal (1:15-1:55)

"正确走法——炮先动！而且不是普通地动，是炮直接冲到中路，献祭！炮往黑方的将头上一放——将军！"
(Correct move — cannon moves first! And not an ordinary move — the cannon charges straight to the center file, a sacrifice! Cannon plants itself right above the Black general — check!)

"黑方怎么办？只能吃掉这个炮。吃了吧？这正是红方要的！因为炮被吃掉的瞬间，中路打开了——红方的车一下子有了进攻的通道！"
(What can Black do? Must capture this cannon. Take it? That's exactly what Red wants! Because the instant the cannon is captured, the center file opens — Red's chariot suddenly has an attack corridor!)

"车从右边杀过来——车九进一，将军！黑将被迫移动。然后马跟上——马到了炮刚才创造的空间里，跳到黑将旁边。车和马开始联手进攻！"
(Chariot swings in from the right — chariot advances, check! The Black general is forced to move. Then the knight follows — into the space the cannon's sacrifice created, jumping next to the Black general. Chariot and knight begin their joint assault!)

"黑方拼命防守——出士、飞象——但是红方的攻击像流水一样，一步接一步，每一步都是连贯的。车将军，马将军，车再将军——黑方顾了这头顾不了那头，最终被车和马合力绝杀！"
(Black defends desperately — advisor out, elephant flies — but Red's attack flows like water, one move after another, each one connected. Chariot checks, knight checks, chariot checks again — Black can't cover everything, and finally chariot and knight combine for checkmate!)

"整盘棋的关键就是第一步的炮！炮牺牲了自己，打开了通道，把机会留给了车和马。这就是'辗丹砂'——炮像碾药一样碾过去，碾出一条血路！"
(The key to the whole puzzle is the cannon's first move! The cannon sacrificed itself, opened the corridor, left the opportunity for chariot and knight. This is 'Rolling Cannon' — the cannon rolls through like a grinding stone, carving a bloody path!)

## Principle (1:55-2:15)

"炮辗丹砂告诉我们：有时候弃子才是最强的进攻。不要舍不得子力——如果牺牲一个子能打开局面，那这个牺牲就是值得的。象棋里最难学的不是怎么吃子，而是怎么弃子。敢弃子，才是真正的高手。"
(Rolling Cannon teaches us: sometimes sacrifice IS the strongest attack. Don't be attached to your pieces — if sacrificing one piece cracks the position open, that sacrifice is worth it. The hardest thing to learn in xiangqi isn't how to capture — it's how to sacrifice. Daring to sacrifice makes you a true master.)
