"从表面上看，红方挺被动的——黑方的马在红方的地盘上，随时能踩一脚。而且黑方的九宫士象齐全，中路防守看似无懈可击。但是——"

## Bad Move #1 (0:35-0:55)

"大部分人的第一反应：先赶走那个过河的黑马！红方走一步把马赶走，稳扎稳打。听起来很稳健对吧？大错特错！你花一步棋去赶马，黑方趁机补防——把士走到更好的位置，或者把象归正。你赶完马回头一看，好家伙，黑方的防守更坚固了！"
(Most people's first instinct: drive away that river-crossing Black knight! Red takes a move to shoo the knight away — steady and safe. Sounds prudent, right? Dead wrong! You spend a move chasing the knight, and Black uses the time to reinforce — moves an advisor to a better position, or repositions an elephant. You chase the knight away, look back, and whoa — Black's defense is even more solid!)

"你帮人家争取了加固城墙的时间！进攻讲究的是——一鼓作气！"
(You gave the enemy time to reinforce the walls! Attack demands — one fierce surge!)

## Bad Move #2 (0:55-1:15)

"第二个错误思路：兵往前拱。红方中兵往前走一步，想着慢慢逼过去。但是这个兵一走，反而挡住了自己炮的攻击线！炮是要隔一个子才能打的，你把兵放在炮前面的位置不对，炮的威力反而减弱了。"
(Second wrong idea: push the pawn forward. Red's center pawn advances one step, thinking of slowly pressing. But this pawn move actually blocks the cannon's attack line! The cannon needs a screen piece to fire, and putting the pawn in the wrong position actually weakens the cannon's power.)

"兵是好子，但放错位置就是绊脚石！"
(Pawns are good pieces, but in the wrong position they're stumbling blocks!)

## Correct Move Reveal (1:15-1:50)

"正确走法——炮直接冲！炮不管黑方的马，不管自己的安全，直接沿着中路往前冲——炮直接打到黑方的九宫心脏位置！"
(Correct move — cannon charges straight in! Ignore the Black knight, ignore its own safety — the cannon fires straight up the center into the heart of Black's palace!)

"炮五进五——穿心炮！炮从中路直接打进去，中间隔着黑方自己的士当炮架——将军！"
(Cannon advances five ranks — Heart Piercer! The cannon fires straight through, using Black's own advisor as the cannon mount — check!)

"黑方懵了。将想往上走？上面炮控制着。想往旁边走？红方的兵在中路堵着，将哪儿都去不了。出士？士一动，炮架没了？不对——炮架变了，变成另一个士或者象，照样将军！"
(Black is stunned. General wants to go up? Cannon controls that. Go sideways? Red's pawn blocks the center — general has nowhere to go. Move an advisor? Advisor moves, cannon mount gone? No — the mount changes to the other advisor or elephant, still check!)

"这就是穿心炮的可怕之处——黑方的士和象本来是防守的，现在全变成了红方炮的炮架！你的防守越厚，我的炮越好用！"
(This is what makes the Heart Piercer terrifying — Black's advisors and elephants were supposed to defend, but now they've all become cannon mounts for Red! The thicker your defense, the better my cannon works!)

"黑方无论怎么走，红方的炮在中宫纹丝不动，车或者兵跟进来收人头——绝杀！"
(No matter what Black does, Red's cannon sits immovably in the center palace. The pawn or other pieces follow up for the kill — checkmate!)

"这一炮穿心——痛快！"
(This heart-piercing cannon shot — exhilarating!)

## Principle (1:50-2:10)

"大胆穿心教给我们：有时候对方的防守棋子反而是你最好的武器。炮这个子很特别，它需要一个炮架才能发威。对方九宫里的士和象就是天然的炮架！所以当你有炮的时候，不要怕对方防守子力多——越多越好，你的炮架越多，攻击角度就越多。还有一个道理：进攻要果断！不要被干扰，不要去管不重要的事情，看准了就打！"
(Bold Heart Piercer teaches us: sometimes the opponent's defensive pieces are your best weapons. The cannon is special — it needs a mount to fire. The advisors and elephants in the opponent's palace are natural cannon mounts! So when you have a cannon, don't fear the opponent's many defensive pieces — the more the better, more mounts mean more attack angles. And another lesson: be decisive in attack! Don't get distracted, don't deal with unimportant things — when you see the opportunity, strike!)
