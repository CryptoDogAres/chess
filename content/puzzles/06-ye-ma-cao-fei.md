# 野马操飞 — Wild Horse Soars

## Video Info
- **Duration**: 2:15 estimated
- **Platform**: Douyin (vertical) + YouTube Shorts
- **Content Type**: 残局谜题 Endgame Puzzle
- **Difficulty**: ★★★★☆
- **FEN**: 3ak4/4a4/4b4/9/9/9/4N4/3RB4/4A4/2BAK4

## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

"一匹马，在九宫外面飞来飞去，黑方的将愣是一点办法都没有——这就是四百年前的古人留给我们的智慧：野马操飞！"
(One knight, soaring around outside the palace — Black's general is completely helpless. This is the wisdom our ancestors left us 400 years ago: Wild Horse Soars!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"这局出自明朝古谱《适情雅趣》。红方有一个车、一匹马。黑方有将，两个士一个象。从子力上看，红方多一个车一个马，优势很大。但是黑方的宫防很结实，士象都在，将也躲得好好的。"
(This comes from the Ming Dynasty manual "Elegant Pastime." Red has a chariot and a knight. Black has the general, two advisors and one elephant. Material-wise, Red is way ahead with a chariot and knight. But Black's palace defense is solid — advisors and elephant all in place, general tucked away safely.)

"问题是：红方怎么攻破这个铁桶一样的防守？"
(The question is: how does Red crack this iron-barrel defense?)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

"你会怎么走？是直接冲车，还是先动马？暂停视频想一想！三、二、一——"
(What would you play? Charge the chariot, or move the knight first? Pause and think! Three, two, one—)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:55)

"大部分人的第一反应：车直接杀进去！车冲到底线，将军！多痛快！但是黑方的士一挡，将往旁边一躲，你的车陷在人家九宫里了。黑方的象还能飞过来助防，你的车左右为难，进退两难。"
(Most people's instinct: chariot charges in! Rush to the back rank, check! How satisfying! But Black's advisor blocks, general dodges to the side — your chariot is stuck inside the palace. Black's elephant can fly over to help defend. Your chariot is stuck — can't advance, can't retreat.)

"车是大子，但冲进对方阵地不一定是好事。有时候，配角才是真正的主角。"
(The chariot is a major piece, but charging into enemy territory isn't always wise. Sometimes, the supporting actor is the real star.)

## Bad Move #2 (0:55-1:15)

"第二个想法：车和马一起往前冲，搞人海战术。马跟着车一起上，想着两个子一起攻总比一个强。但问题是——马跟在车后面，反而挡住了车的退路。黑方走一步将，红车不得不让开，整个进攻节奏就乱了。"
(Second idea: charge both the chariot and knight together, strength in numbers. Knight follows the chariot, thinking two pieces attacking is better than one. But the problem — the knight behind the chariot actually blocks the chariot's retreat. Black plays one check, Red's chariot has to move aside, and the whole attack rhythm falls apart.)

"子力配合不是挤在一块儿，而是各司其职！"
(Piece coordination isn't about crowding together — it's about each piece having its own role!)

## Correct Move Reveal (1:15-1:50)

"正确的走法——马先飞！不是往前冲，而是往侧面跳。马跳到一个看起来离黑将很远的位置。很多人会说：你这马跳那么远干什么？别急，看下去！"
(The correct move — knight soars first! Not charging forward, but jumping to the side. The knight leaps to a position that seems far from the Black general. Many would ask: why is the knight going so far away? Patience — keep watching!)

"马到了侧面之后，下一步再跳回来——这次跳到黑方九宫的要害位置！黑方的士被马踩住了，将的退路被封死。这就叫'操飞'——马在外面绕了一圈，飞到了最致命的位置！"
(After reaching the flank, the knight jumps back in — this time landing on a critical square in Black's palace! Black's advisor is pinned under the knight, the general's retreat is sealed. This is 'soaring' — the knight circles outside, then flies to the deadliest position!)

"这时候车再上来——车直接冲进去，将军！黑将想跑？左右都被马控制了。想出士？士已经被马踩住了。绝杀！"
(Now the chariot moves in — chariot charges straight in, check! General wants to run? Left and right controlled by the knight. Move an advisor? The advisor is pinned by the knight. Checkmate!)

"妙啊！马绕了一个大圈子，看似浪费时间，实际上是在找最佳攻击角度。这就是野马操飞的精髓——不走直线，走曲线，曲线救国！"
(Brilliant! The knight took a big detour — seems like wasted time, but it was actually finding the optimal attack angle. This is the essence of Wild Horse Soars — don't go straight, take the curve. The indirect path wins!)

## Principle (1:50-2:10)

"野马操飞教给我们：马的迂回战术。马不像车能走直线，马的走法本来就是拐弯的。所以用马的时候不要急着往前冲，有时候先跳到旁边，再绕回来，反而能到达一个车都去不了的位置。记住——马走曲线，曲线也是进攻！"
(Wild Horse Soars teaches us: the knight's flanking tactics. Unlike chariots that go in straight lines, the knight naturally moves in angles. So when using the knight, don't rush forward. Sometimes jumping to the side first, then circling back, actually reaches a position even a chariot can't access. Remember — the knight takes curved paths, and curves are attacks too!)
