# 弃子归边 — Sacrifice to the Flank

## Video Info
- **Duration**: 2:05 estimated
- **Platform**: Douyin (vertical) + YouTube Shorts
- **Content Type**: 残局谜题 Endgame Puzzle
- **Difficulty**: ★★★☆☆
- **FEN**: 2bak4/4a4/4b4/p8/4N4/P1B6/9/2R6/4A4/2BAK4

## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

"黑方贪吃了一个子，结果边路大门洞开——三步之内被将死！弃子归边，这招你一定要学会！"
(Black greedily captures a piece, and the flank door swings wide open — checkmate in three moves! Sacrifice to the Flank — you MUST learn this move!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"看这个局面。红方有一个车在左边，一匹马在中间。黑方将在中间，士象齐全，左边还有一个卒。现在的局面，黑方看起来很安全——中路有士象，边上有卒顶着。红方好像攻不进去。"
(Look at this position. Red has a chariot on the left, a knight in the center. Black's general is in the middle, advisors and elephants all present, plus a pawn on the left. The position — Black looks safe. Center has advisors and elephants, the flank has a pawn holding. Red seems stuck.)

"但是红方有一招妙手——先弃再攻！"
(But Red has a brilliant move — sacrifice first, attack second!)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

"红方应该先动哪个子？怎么走？暂停视频想一想！三、二、一——"
(Which piece should Red move first? How? Pause and think! Three, two, one—)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:50)

"第一个错误：车直接冲到边路去进攻。车走到边线上，想着从侧面攻进九宫。但黑方的象守着呢！象飞过来一挡，车被堵在外面。而且红方的马在中路看热闹，一点忙都帮不上。"
(First mistake: chariot charges straight to the flank. Chariot goes to the edge file, trying to attack the palace from the side. But Black's elephant is guarding! Elephant flies over to block, chariot stuck outside. And Red's knight is in the center watching the show, no help at all.)

"直接进攻有时候就像蛮牛冲撞——看着凶，其实没用。"
(Direct attack is sometimes like a bull charging — looks fierce, actually useless.)

## Bad Move #2 (0:50-1:10)

"第二个错误思路：马跳过去将军，逼黑方应对。马踩到九宫附近，将军。看起来很主动？但是黑方走士，挡住了。然后你的马陷在人家阵地里，被黑方的士象围着，车又上不来配合——马反而成了一个靶子。"
(Second wrong idea: knight jumps in to check, forcing Black to respond. Knight lands near the palace, check. Looks proactive? But Black plays an advisor, blocks it. Then your knight is stuck in enemy territory, surrounded by Black's advisors and elephants. The chariot can't coordinate — the knight becomes a target instead.)

## Correct Move Reveal (1:10-1:45)

"正确走法——马不去将军，马去送死！马往黑方的边路一跳，直接送到黑方卒的嘴里。黑方一看：嘿，白送一匹马？吃了！卒吃马。"
(Correct move — the knight doesn't go check, the knight goes to die! Knight jumps toward Black's flank, offering itself right to the pawn's mouth. Black sees it: hey, a free knight? Capture! Pawn takes knight.)

"但是黑方这一吃，就上当了！卒离开了它原来的位置，边路一下子空了！红方的车早就等着呢——车从左边直冲到底线，杀到黑方九宫的侧面！"
(But by capturing, Black fell for the trap! The pawn left its original position — the flank is suddenly wide open! Red's chariot has been waiting — chariot charges straight up the left side to the back rank, right next to Black's palace!)

"车到了边路底线，直接将军！黑将想逃？往中间走——但是中路已经被红方的兵和象控制住了。黑方只能出士来挡，但挡了一步之后——车再进一步，再将军！黑方已经没有棋子可以挡了。绝杀！"
(Chariot on the flank's back rank, check! General wants to escape? Move to center — but the center is already controlled by Red's pawn and elephant. Black can only play an advisor to block, but after one block — chariot advances again, check again! Black has no more pieces to block. Checkmate!)

"看到了吗？那匹马死得其所——它用自己的牺牲，撬开了边路的大门，让车杀了进去。这就是弃子归边！"
(See that? The knight died a worthy death — its sacrifice pried open the flank door, letting the chariot charge in. This is Sacrifice to the Flank!)

## Principle (1:45-2:00)

"弃子归边教我们一个道理：不要贪吃！黑方如果不吃那个马，马也没什么大用。但黑方一贪，就把自己的防线拆了。所以记住——对方送子给你吃的时候，先想想他为什么要送。天下没有免费的午餐，棋盘上也没有白送的子！"
(Sacrifice to the Flank teaches us: don't be greedy! If Black doesn't capture that knight, the knight is harmless. But Black got greedy and dismantled his own defense. So remember — when the opponent offers you a piece, first ask why. There's no free lunch in the world, and no free pieces on the board!)
