## Opening Hook (0:00-0:05)

(What's the most stylish checkmate in xiangqi? 90% of players will tell you — the Knight-Backed Cannon!)

## Position Setup (0:05-0:20)

"来看这个局面。红方有一匹马，站在中路，还有一门炮，在底线等着呢。黑方呢？将在中间，两个士守着，看起来很安全。但是——真的安全吗？"
(Look at this position. Red has a knight in the center, and a cannon waiting on the back rank. Black? The general is in the middle, two advisors guarding — looks safe. But is it really?)

"红方的马在宫前虎视眈眈，炮在下面蓄势待发。关键问题是：炮应该走到哪里？"
(Red's knight is eyeing the palace, the cannon is ready below. The key question: where should the cannon go?)

## Interactive Pause (0:20-0:30)

## Bad Move #1 (0:30-0:50)

"很多朋友第一反应是：炮走中路，直接将军！炮六进五，看起来很凶对不对？但是你忘了一件事——黑方的士可以挡！士五进四，炮被挡住了，马也没有配合到位。红方白白浪费了一步棋，黑方反而可以出车反击。"
(Many players instinctively go: cannon to the center file, check! Cannon advances — looks fierce right? But you forgot something — Black's advisor can block! Advisor moves, cannon is blocked, the knight isn't coordinating. Red wastes a move, and Black can counterattack with the chariot.)

"这就是心急吃不了热豆腐啊！"
(Haste makes waste!)

## Correct Move Reveal (0:50-1:30)

"正确的走法是什么呢？注意看——马先跳！马进到将的斜前方，踩住中心位置。马到了这里，直接控制住了黑将的两个逃跑路线。"
(What's the correct move? Watch carefully — the knight jumps first! Knight moves to the diagonal in front of the general, controlling the center. From here, the knight blocks both escape routes for the Black general.)

"黑方没办法，只能走将。将往旁边躲——但是来不及了！"
(Black has no choice, must move the general. The general dodges to the side — but it's too late!)

"炮！炮从后面一打过来——砰！马在前面挡住去路，炮从后面将军，中间隔着黑方自己的棋子当炮架。黑将无处可逃，绝杀！"
(Cannon! The cannon fires from behind — boom! The knight blocks the escape in front, cannon checks from behind, using Black's own piece as the cannon mount. The general has nowhere to run — checkmate!)

"这就是马后炮！马在前，炮在后，完美配合！"
(This is the Knight-Backed Cannon! Knight in front, cannon behind, perfect coordination!)

## Principle (1:30-1:45)

"马后炮教给我们一个道理：子力配合比单打独斗重要一百倍。炮一个人再厉害，没有马在前面卡位，也没法杀棋。下棋的时候，永远要想：我的子力怎么配合？"
(The Knight-Backed Cannon teaches us: coordination is a hundred times more important than individual piece power. No matter how strong the cannon is, without the knight blocking the escape, there's no checkmate. When you play, always ask: how can my pieces work together?)
