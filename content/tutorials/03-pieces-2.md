## 详细大纲 Detailed Outline

### 第一节：兵/卒——不起眼的小兵 (Pawn — The Humble Foot Soldier) — 约3分钟

**核心要点 Key Talking Points:**

1. **兵怎么走 (How the Pawn Moves)**
   - 没过河之前：只能向前走一步，不能后退，不能横走
   - (Before crossing the river: can only move forward one step; no retreating, no sideways.)
   - 过河之后：可以向前走一步，也可以横走一步——但仍然不能后退
   - (After crossing the river: can move forward OR sideways one step — but still no retreating.)
   - 注意：象棋的兵没有国际象棋那种"升变"——过了河还是兵
   - (Note: Chinese chess Pawns don't "promote" like in international chess — a Pawn stays a Pawn.)

2. **兵怎么吃 (How the Pawn Captures)**
   - 兵的吃法和走法完全一样——走到对方棋子位置就吃掉
   - (The Pawn captures the same way it moves — step onto the opponent's piece.)
   - 不像国际象棋的兵斜着吃——象棋的兵直着走也直着吃
   - (Unlike the international chess Pawn's diagonal capture — the Chinese chess Pawn captures straight ahead.)

3. **小兵的价值 (The Pawn's Value)**
   - 没过河的兵价值很低（约1分），过了河的兵价值翻倍（约2分）
   - (An uncrossed Pawn is worth very little (~1 point); a crossed Pawn doubles in value (~2 points).)
   - 到了底线的兵只能横走——叫"老兵"，价值又降低了
   - (A Pawn on the last rank can only move sideways — called an "old Pawn," its value drops again.)

**示例棋局 Example Position 1 — 兵过河前后的对比：**

```
FEN: 4k4/9/9/9/9/4P4/9/9/9/4K4 w - - 0 1
```
（红兵在河边，准备过河。过河后可以横走，活动范围变大。）
(Red Pawn at the river's edge, about to cross. After crossing, it can move sideways — expanded range.)

### 第二节：士/仕——将帅的贴身侍卫 (Advisor — The King's Bodyguard) — 约2.5分钟

**核心要点 Key Talking Points:**

1. **士怎么走 (How the Advisor Moves)**
   - 士只能在九宫格里面活动
   - (The Advisor can only move within the palace.)
   - 每次走一步，只能走斜线（对角线方向）
   - (One step at a time, diagonals only.)
   - 九宫格里一共只有5个点可以站：4个角 + 1个中心
   - (There are only 5 positions available in the palace: 4 corners + 1 center.)

2. **士的作用 (The Advisor's Role)**
   - 士是防守型棋子，主要任务是保护将/帅
   - (The Advisor is defensive — its main job is to protect the King.)
   - 两个士配合可以形成稳固的防守——"士角"是个关键位置
   - (Two Advisors together form solid defense — the "Advisor corner" is a key position.)
   - 不要轻易丢士——没有士的保护，将帅很容易被杀
   - (Don't lose Advisors carelessly — without their protection, the King is easily checkmated.)

3. **士的价值 (The Advisor's Value)**
   - 大约2分——比过河兵略强
   - (About 2 points — slightly stronger than a crossed Pawn.)

**示例棋局 Example Position 2 — 士的防守位置：**

```
FEN: 3ak4/4a4/9/9/9/9/9/9/9/3AKA3 w - - 0 1
```
（双方的士在标准防守位置）
(Both sides' Advisors in standard defensive positions.)

### 第三节：象/相——不能过河的大象 (Elephant — The Big Piece That Can't Cross the River) — 约3分钟

**核心要点 Key Talking Points:**

1. **象怎么走 (How the Elephant Moves)**
   - 象走"田"字形：沿对角线走两格
   - (The Elephant moves in a "field character" shape: two steps diagonally.)
   - 可以到达的点形成一个菱形，每次跳到菱形的一个角
   - (The reachable points form a diamond; each move jumps to one corner of the diamond.)

2. **塞象眼！(Blocking the Elephant's Eye!)**
   - 象走的"田"字中心如果有棋子，象就走不了——这叫"塞象眼"
   - (If a piece occupies the center of the "field" path, the Elephant is blocked — called "plugging the Elephant's eye.")
   - 和马的"蹩马腿"类似，都是中间有棋子就不能走
   - (Similar to the Horse's blocking rule — a piece in the middle prevents the move.)

3. **象不能过河 (The Elephant Cannot Cross the River)**
   - 这是象最大的限制——只能在自己这半边棋盘活动
   - (This is the Elephant's biggest limitation — it can only operate on its own half of the board.)
   - 所以象是纯防守棋子，不能参与进攻
   - (So the Elephant is purely defensive — it can't join the attack.)
   - 红方叫"相"，黑方叫"象"——名字不同，走法一样
   - (Red's is called "相", Black's is called "象" — different names, same movement.)

4. **象的价值 (The Elephant's Value)**
   - 大约2分——和士差不多
   - (About 2 points — similar to the Advisor.)
   - 象和士配合防守效果最好
   - (Elephants and Advisors together create the best defense.)

**示例棋局 Example Position 3 — 塞象眼演示：**

```
FEN: 4k4/9/9/9/9/9/9/2n1E4/9/4K4 w - - 0 1
```
（红象在e3想走到c1，但d2如果有子就被塞象眼）
(Red Elephant on e3 wants to move to c1, but if a piece is on d2 it's blocked.)

### 第四节：帅/将——全军的灵魂 (King — The Soul of the Army) — 约2.5分钟

**核心要点 Key Talking Points:**

1. **帅怎么走 (How the King Moves)**
   - 帅只能在九宫格里面活动
   - (The King can only move within the palace.)
   - 每次走一步，只能走直线（上下左右），不能走斜线
   - (One step at a time, straight lines only (up/down/left/right), no diagonals.)
   - 注意：这和国际象棋的国王不一样——象棋的帅不能走斜线
   - (Note: unlike the international chess King, the Chinese chess King cannot move diagonally.)

2. **对面笑规则 (Face-to-Face Rule)**
   - 两个帅/将不能在同一条竖线上面对面，中间没有任何棋子
   - (The two Kings cannot face each other on the same vertical line with no pieces in between.)
   - 如果你走了一步导致两个将帅面对面了——这步棋不合法
   - (If your move causes the two Kings to face each other — that move is illegal.)
   - 这个规则非常重要！残局中经常用到
   - (This rule is extremely important! It's frequently used in endgames.)
   - 俗称"对面笑"——好像两个国王面对面笑了
   - (Colloquially called "face-to-face laugh" — as if two Kings are laughing at each other.)

3. **帅的价值 (The King's Value)**
   - 帅是无价的——丢了帅就输了
   - (The King is invaluable — lose the King and you lose the game.)
   - 所以帅不参与交换，我们不给帅打分
   - (So the King isn't traded; we don't assign it a point value.)

**示例棋局 Example Position 4 — 对面笑规则：**

```
FEN: 4k4/9/9/9/9/9/9/9/9/4K4 w - - 0 1
```
（两个将帅在同一竖线，中间无子——不合法！红方不能走到这个位置。）
(Two Kings on the same file with nothing between — illegal! Red cannot make a move resulting in this.)

**示例棋局 Example Position 5 — 利用对面笑取胜：**

```
FEN: 3k5/4R4/9/9/9/9/9/9/9/3K5 w - - 0 1
```
（红帅在d1，红车在e9。黑将不能走到e9因为会和红帅对面。利用这个规则限制对方将的移动。）
(Red King on d1, Red Chariot on e9. The Black King can't move to certain squares due to the face-to-face rule — use this to limit the opponent's King.)

## 练习题 Practice Puzzle

**问题：下面这个局面，轮到红方走。红方能利用"对面笑"规则获胜吗？**
(Question: It's Red's turn. Can Red use the "face-to-face" rule to win?)

```
FEN: 4k4/3Ra4/9/9/9/9/9/9/9/4K4 w - - 0 1
```

**提示 Hint:** 如果红车吃掉黑士，黑将能不能吃回来？想想对面笑的规则！
(If the Red Chariot captures the Black Advisor, can the Black King recapture? Think about the face-to-face rule!)

**答案 Answer:** 红车吃士（d9吃e9），黑将不能吃回来——因为吃回来就和红帅对面了！所以红方下一步车吃将，获胜。
(Chariot captures the Advisor on e9. The Black King cannot recapture — doing so would face the Red King directly! So next move, Chariot takes the King. Red wins.)

## 本集总结 Key Takeaways

1. **兵/卒** 只能前进不能后退，过河后可以横走
   (Pawns can only advance, never retreat; after crossing the river they can move sideways.)
2. **士/仕** 走斜线，只能在九宫格里，是防守支柱
   (Advisors move diagonally, palace only — defensive pillars.)
3. **象/相** 走"田"字，不能过河，注意"塞象眼"
   (Elephants move in a "field" pattern, can't cross the river, watch for "blocking the eye.")
4. **帅/将** 在九宫格走直线，记住"对面笑"规则
   (Kings move straight in the palace; remember the "face-to-face" rule.)
