
**问题：红方能在两步之内将杀黑方吗？请找出完整的杀法。**
(Question: Can Red checkmate Black in two moves? Find the complete sequence.)

**提示 Hint:** 想想我们在第4集学过的马后炮——但需要先用一步棋创造条件。
(Think about the Horse-behind-Cannon from Episode 4 — but you need one preparatory move first.)

**答案 Answer:**
1. 马e8进d10（将军！）黑将必须走d10或e10
   (Horse e8 to d10 — check! Black King must go to d10 or e10.)
2. 如果黑将走e10，红炮e3走e9——马后炮将杀！马在d10做炮架。
   (If Black King goes to e10, Red Cannon to e9 — Horse-behind-Cannon checkmate! Horse on d10 as platform.)
   如果黑将走d9，红炮e3走e9也是将杀——因为马控制了所有逃路。
   (If Black King goes to d9, Red Cannon to e9 is also checkmate — Horse controls all escape squares.)

完美的结局——我们用第4集学的马后炮完成了最后的将杀！

## 本集总结 Key Takeaways

1. **一盘完整的棋分三个阶段**：开局（出子）→ 中局（战术）→ 残局（精确计算）
   (A complete game has three phases: Opening (development) → Midgame (tactics) → Endgame (precise calculation).)
2. **开局重点**：快速出子、控制中路、不贪吃
   (Opening focus: rapid development, center control, don't be greedy.)
3. **中局重点**：运用战术（抽将、捉双等）、子力配合
   (Midgame focus: apply tactics (discovered checks, forks, etc.), piece coordination.)
4. **残局重点**：耐心推进、帅要参战、记住马强炮弱
   (Endgame focus: patient progress, activate King, remember Horse > Cannon.)
5. **最重要的**：多下棋，多思考，享受象棋的乐趣！
   (Most important: play more, think more, enjoy the fun of Chinese chess!)
