# Complete Douyin Channel Setup Guide

A step-by-step guide to creating, optimizing, and growing a xiangqi (Chinese chess) channel on Douyin (抖音). Every setting, dimension, and strategy you need is included below.

---

## 1. Account Creation

1. Download the Douyin app (iOS App Store or Android official site: douyin.com).
2. Open the app and tap **"我" (Me)** in the bottom-right corner.
3. Register with a Chinese phone number (+86). If you are outside mainland China, you can use a virtual Chinese number service or register via a linked WeChat/QQ account.
4. Complete SMS verification.
5. Skip the initial interest selection or pick **棋牌** (board games) and **教育** (education) to seed your recommendation profile.

### Switching to a Creator Account (创作者账号)

1. Go to **我 → 右上角三横线 → 设置 → 账号与安全 → 切换为创作者账号**.
2. Select category: **教育** → **兴趣教学** or **文化** → **传统文化**.
3. A creator account unlocks analytics (创作者服务中心), live streaming eligibility, and the ability to add a contact email to your profile.

## 2. Profile Optimization

### Display Name (昵称)

- Maximum 20 characters.
- Include a keyword for discoverability. Recommended formats:
  - `象棋[YourName]` — e.g., 象棋老王
  - `[YourName]讲象棋` — e.g., 大明讲象棋
  - `[YourName]·象棋残局` — niche-focused

### Avatar (头像)

- **Dimensions:** 400 x 400 px minimum (displayed as a circle).
- **Format:** JPG or PNG, under 5 MB.
- Use a clear headshot or a branded logo with a chess piece motif. High contrast and warm tones perform best in the circular crop.

### Banner / Background Image (背景图)

- **Dimensions:** 1125 x 633 px (displayed at the top of your profile).
- **Format:** JPG or PNG.
- Include your channel name, posting schedule, and a visual related to xiangqi (board layout, piece close-up). Keep key content in the center 750 x 400 safe area to avoid cropping on different devices.

### Bio (简介)

Maximum 100 characters. Include: what you do, posting frequency, and a call to action.

**Variant 1 — Professional:**

```
20年棋龄，专注中局战术与残局技巧
每晚8点更新 · 关注不迷路
```

**Variant 2 — Casual / Approachable:**
```
一个爱下棋的普通人，陪你每天进步一点点
残局拆解 · 实战复盘 · 新手教学
```

**Variant 3 — Bilingual (for international reach):**

### Additional Profile Fields

- **Location:** Set to your city for local recommendation boost.
- **Gender:** Optional but setting it increases profile completeness score.
- **Contact email:** Add a business email under creator settings for brand deals.

## 3. Content Settings and Video Specs

### Vertical Video (Primary Format)

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1080 x 1920 px |
| Aspect Ratio | 9:16 |
| Frame Rate | 30 fps (60 fps for fast-paced live replays) |
| Codec | H.264 or H.265 |
| Bitrate | 6-10 Mbps for 1080p |
| Audio | AAC, 128 kbps minimum, 44.1 kHz |
| Max Duration | 15 min (verified accounts up to 30 min) |
| Max File Size | 4 GB |

### Horizontal Video (Secondary)

- 1920 x 1080 at 16:9 is supported but will display with black bars in the feed.
- If repurposing horizontal content, use 剪映 (CapCut China edition) to add a blurred background fill or top/bottom context bars to fill the 9:16 frame.

### Cover Image (封面)

- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px (same as video).
- Use large bold text (大粗字) in the center third of the frame.
- Recommended font size: 80-120pt equivalent.
- High contrast: white or yellow text on a dark background, or vice versa.

## 4. Going Live on Douyin (直播)

### Eligibility Threshold

- **1,000 followers** minimum to unlock the live streaming feature.
- Account must be in good standing (no community guideline strikes in the past 30 days).

### How to Start a Live Stream

1. Open Douyin → tap the **"+" button** at the bottom center → swipe to **开直播**.
2. Choose **视频直播** (video live) for standard streaming.
3. Set a title (max 20 characters): e.g., "残局挑战赛·你来我解" or "实战对弈·评论区点将".
4. Select a cover image (pre-made 1080x1920).
5. Tap **开始视频直播**.

### OBS / Third-Party Streaming (推流)

For higher-quality streams using OBS or other software:

1. Go to **创作者服务中心 → 开始直播 → PC直播** (on the Douyin creator web portal or 抖音直播伴侣 app).
2. Copy the **RTMP Server URL** and **Stream Key** provided.
3. In OBS: Settings → Stream → Service: Custom → paste the server URL and stream key.
4. Recommended OBS output settings for Douyin:
   - Encoder: x264 or NVENC
   - Bitrate: 4000–6000 kbps
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical) or 1920x1080 (horizontal)
   - Keyframe interval: 2 seconds

## 7. Understanding the Douyin Algorithm (抖音算法)

Douyin uses a tiered distribution model. Every video enters a small initial pool (~200–500 views). Performance at each tier determines promotion to the next.

### Key Ranking Factors (in order of weight)

1. **Completion Rate (完播率):** The single most important metric. A 3-minute video watched to 100% by most viewers will massively outperform a 10-minute video with 30% average completion.
   - Target: >40% for videos under 60 seconds, >25% for videos 1–3 minutes.
2. **Likes (点赞率):** Target >5% like-to-view ratio.
3. **Comments (评论率):** Target >1% comment-to-view ratio. Ask questions at the end to drive comments.
4. **Shares (转发率):** Shares to WeChat or within Douyin signal high value. Puzzles that viewers want to share with friends perform exceptionally well.
5. **Follows from video (关注转化):** New followers gained from a single video boost its distribution.
6. **Replay rate (重复播放):** Viewers rewatching = strong positive signal.
7. **Profile visit rate:** Viewers clicking to your profile after watching.

### Actionable Takeaways

- Hook viewers in the first 1–2 seconds. Use a bold on-screen statement or an unexpected board position.
- Keep puzzle videos under 60 seconds for maximum completion rate.
- End every video with a question or challenge: "你能看出下一步吗？评论区见。"
- Reply to every comment in the first hour after posting to boost the comment count and engagement rate.

## 8. Monetization

### Gifts and Tips During Live Streams (直播打赏)

- Viewers can send virtual gifts (抖币) during live streams.
- Revenue split: creators receive approximately 50% of gift value after Douyin's commission.
- Enable gifts in: 创作者服务中心 → 直播 → 收益管理.
- Minimum withdrawal: 50 RMB. Withdrawal to linked bank card or Alipay.

### Douyin E-commerce (抖音小店)

- Requirements: Business license or individual merchant registration, 2,000+ followers.
- Sell xiangqi boards, books, or courses directly through your videos using product links.

### 巨量星图 (Massive Star Map — Brand Collaboration Platform)

- **Registration requirements:**
  - 10,000+ followers.
  - Active creator account with consistent posting history (at least 30 days).
  - Account in good standing.
- Register at: star.toutiao.com.
- Set your pricing for video placements, live stream mentions, and custom content.
- Xiangqi channels can attract brands in: educational apps, board game manufacturers, tea brands, traditional culture products.

### Douyin Creator Fund (中视频伙伴计划)

- For videos longer than 1 minute.
- Revenue based on view count and engagement quality.
- Enroll in 创作者服务中心 → 任务中心.

## 9. Growth Hacks Specific to Douyin

### 1. Series Feature (合集)

Create a series (合集) for structured content like "30天象棋入门" or "经典残局100题." Series appear as a dedicated section on your profile and auto-link videos for binge-watching, which dramatically increases total watch time.

### 2. Duet and Stitch (合拍 / 抢镜)

React to other xiangqi creators' content. This exposes you to their audience. E.g., "我来解这道残局" as a duet with a popular puzzle video.

### 3. Comment Seeding

Be the first commenter on your own videos with a follow-up question or additional insight. Pin this comment. Other users replying to it boosts the comment tree.

### 4. Cross-Promote from Search (搜索流量)

Douyin search is growing. Include searchable terms in your video description: "象棋残局 马后炮杀法 教学" — natural language phrases people type into search.

### 5. Trending Audio

When a trending audio clip is relevant (e.g., dramatic music for a critical move), use it. Trending audio gets a distribution boost.

### 6. Post Frequency

During the first 30 days, post 1–2 times per day to help the algorithm understand your niche. After establishing a baseline, 4–7 posts per week is sustainable.

### 7. DOU+ (Paid Promotion)

- Douyin's native ad tool. Minimum spend: 100 RMB.
- Best used on videos that already show organic traction (>5% like rate in the first hour).
- Target by interest: 棋牌, 传统文化, 益智游戏.
- Promote for follower growth (关注) rather than pure views for long-term value.

### 8. Collaborate with Other Creators

Find xiangqi creators at a similar follower count and do 对弈直播 (live match) together. Both audiences get exposed to the other creator.

### 9. Leverage Holidays and Events

Post themed content around major xiangqi events (全国象棋甲级联赛, 象棋个人赛) and Chinese holidays (Spring Festival, Mid-Autumn) when platform traffic spikes.

### 10. Analytics Review Cadence

Check 创作者服务中心 → 数据中心 weekly. Track:
- Average completion rate trend
- Follower demographics (age, gender, location)
- Traffic sources (推荐页 vs 搜索 vs 关注页)
- Best-performing content topics for doubling down
