# 天天象棋 Setup and Usage Guide for Content Creators

A practical guide to using Tencent's 天天象棋 (Tiantian Xiangqi) app for playing, analyzing, and generating content for your xiangqi channel. Covers download, setup, key features, content ideas, mobile recording techniques, and workflow integration with other tools.

---

## 1. Download and Installation

天天象棋 is developed by Tencent and is the most popular xiangqi app in China with over 200 million downloads. It is free to download with optional in-app purchases.

### iOS

1. Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Search for "天天象棋" (or "Tiantian Chess" — the official English listing may vary).
3. Download the app published by Tencent Mobile Games (腾讯移动游戏).
4. The app is approximately 400-500 MB. Ensure you have sufficient storage.

### Android

1. Open your device's app store. On Chinese Android devices, use the pre-installed store (华为应用市场, 小米应用商店, OPPO 软件商店, etc.).
2. Search for "天天象棋".
3. Alternatively, download from the Tencent MyApp store (应用宝) at myapp.com.
4. If using Google Play (outside mainland China), the app may not be available. Use a Chinese app store or download the APK from the official Tencent games site.

### System requirements

| Platform | Minimum Version | Recommended |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| iOS | iOS 11.0+ | iOS 15.0+ for smooth recording |
| Android | Android 5.0+ | Android 10.0+ |
| Storage | 500 MB free | 1 GB free (for replay storage) |

## 2. Account Setup

### Login options

天天象棋 supports three login methods:

**WeChat login (微信登录) — Recommended**
- Tap the WeChat icon on the login screen.
- Authorize the app in WeChat.
- Your WeChat avatar and nickname become your in-game identity.
- This is the most common login method and connects you to the largest player pool.

**QQ login (QQ登录)**
- Tap the QQ icon on the login screen.
- Log in with your QQ account credentials.
- Useful if your gaming identity is tied to QQ.

**Guest login (游客登录)**
- Play without an account. Progress is not saved across devices.
- Not recommended for content creators — you need persistent rating data and game history.

### Initial setup after login

1. **Set your display name (昵称):** Choose a name that matches or references your Douyin channel name for cross-platform recognition. If your Douyin name is "象棋老王," use "象棋老王" or "老王讲棋" in the app.
2. **Set your avatar:** Use your channel avatar or a recognizable image. Viewers watching your screen recordings will see this.
3. **Join a club (棋社):** Optional but useful for networking. Search for active clubs and join one to access club tournaments and chat.

## 3. Key Features for Content Creators

天天象棋 is not just a playing app — it is a content creation toolkit. Here are the features most relevant to building your channel.

### 3.1 棋力评测 (Rating Test)

**What it is:** A calibration system that estimates your playing strength by having you play a series of rated games against opponents of varying difficulty. The result is a numerical rating displayed on your profile.

**Why it matters for content:**
- Your rating is social proof. Displaying a high rating builds credibility with your audience.
- Rating milestones make natural content: "My journey from 1200 to 2000 rating" is a compelling series arc.
- Viewers will ask about your rating. Having it visible in the app provides instant proof.

**How to access:**
1. From the main menu, tap 棋力评测.
2. Play the assigned games. The system typically requires 5-10 games for initial calibration.
3. Your rating appears on your profile card after calibration.

**Content tip:** Record your initial rating test as your first piece of content from this app. The stakes are real, the games are unpredictable, and viewers love watching someone "prove themselves."

### 3.2 智能复盘 (AI Review / Smart Analysis)

**What it is:** After completing a game, the app provides an AI-powered analysis that evaluates each move, identifies mistakes, suggests better alternatives, and assigns a move quality score (excellent, good, inaccuracy, mistake, blunder).

**Why it matters for content:**
- It provides a ready-made narrative structure for analysis videos: walk through the game, pause at the AI-flagged moments, and explain what happened.
- The visual move quality indicators (color-coded moves) are immediately understandable on screen.
- You can compare your moves against the engine's suggestions, which creates natural teaching moments.

**How to access:**
1. After a game ends, tap 复盘 (Review) on the result screen.
2. Select 智能复盘 for the AI analysis mode.
3. The app processes the game and displays a move-by-move evaluation with scores.
4. You can step through moves and see the engine's preferred line at each point.

**Content tip:** Use AI Review for post-game analysis videos. The format is simple: "I played this game, here is what the AI says I did wrong, and here is what I should have done." This works for both your own games and viewer-submitted games.

### 3.3 残局闯关 (Endgame Puzzle Stages)

**What it is:** A collection of 500+ endgame puzzles organized by difficulty, presented as "stages" (关卡) you progress through sequentially. Each puzzle presents a board position where you must find the winning moves.

**Why it matters for content:**
- This is a content goldmine. Each puzzle is a self-contained 1-3 minute video.
- The staged progression creates natural series content: "Puzzle Stage 1-10," "Stage 50 Challenge," etc.
- Some puzzles are famously difficult, creating dramatic moments when you solve (or fail to solve) them.
- Viewers can follow along and attempt the same puzzles, driving app downloads and engagement.

**How to access:**
1. From the main menu, tap 残局闯关.
2. Start from Stage 1 or select any unlocked stage.
3. Solve the puzzle by finding the correct sequence of moves.
4. The app provides hints if you get stuck (but avoid using hints on camera — struggling is better content).

**Content formats:**

| Format | Description | Typical Length |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Speed-run challenge | Solve as many puzzles as possible in a time limit | 5-10 minutes |
| Blind attempt | Try to solve without preparation, explain thinking | 3-5 minutes per puzzle |
| Teaching walkthrough | Solve and explain the endgame principles behind the puzzle | 5-8 minutes per puzzle |
| Viewer challenge | Let viewers suggest a stage number, attempt it live | Varies |

### 3.4 在线对弈 (Online Play)

**What it is:** Real-time matchmaking against other players. The app matches you with opponents of similar rating, and you play with a time control (typically 10-15 minutes per side for standard games, or 3-5 minutes for blitz).

**Why it matters for content:**
- Live play with commentary is one of the highest-engagement content formats in chess.
- The matchmaking system ensures you face opponents at your level, producing competitive games.
- The variety of opponents means no two games are alike — infinite content supply.
- Blitz games create high-energy, fast-paced content ideal for short-form video.

**How to access:**
1. From the main menu, tap 在线对弈.
2. Select your preferred time control: 快棋 (blitz), 慢棋 (standard), or 好友对战 (friend match).
3. Tap 开始匹配 (Start matching) and wait for an opponent (usually under 30 seconds).
4. Play the game.

**Content tip:** For streaming, standard time control gives you more time to explain your thinking. For short-form video, use blitz games and add commentary in post-production. The friend match feature lets you arrange games with other content creators for collaboration content.

### 3.5 棋谱收藏 (Game Collection)

**What it is:** A personal library where your played games and favorite positions are automatically saved. You can browse, replay, and share any game from your history.

**Why it matters for content:**
- Never lose a game you want to analyze later. Every online game is automatically stored.
- You can revisit games days or weeks later when you need content ideas.
- Share game records with viewers or collaborators using the built-in sharing function.
- Export game records for analysis in external tools (see Section 6).

**How to access:**
1. From the main menu, tap 棋谱收藏 or 对局记录.
2. Browse your game history sorted by date.
3. Tap any game to replay it move by move.
4. Use the share button to export the game record.

## 4. Content Ideas from the App

Here are proven content formats built around 天天象棋 features.

### Series 1: "Climbing the Rating Ladder" (棋力晋升之路)

**Concept:** Document your journey climbing through rating tiers. Each episode covers a session of rated games with analysis of key moments.

**Structure per episode:**
1. Show current rating at the start.
2. Play 3-5 rated games, recording key moments.
3. Review the best or worst game using 智能复盘.
4. Show rating change at the end.
5. Close with what you learned this session.

**Why it works:** Progress narratives are inherently compelling. Viewers root for you to reach the next tier. The rating number provides concrete, measurable progress that makes each episode feel different.

### Series 2: "Can I Beat This Puzzle?" (残局挑战)

**Concept:** Attempt endgame puzzles live with no preparation. React authentically. Celebrate when you solve them, analyze when you fail.

**Structure per episode:**
1. Open 残局闯关, show the stage number.
2. Study the position for 30 seconds, explain first impressions.
3. Attempt the solution, narrating your thought process.
4. If you solve it: explain why the solution works and the underlying endgame principle.
5. If you fail: show the correct answer and explain what you missed.

**Why it works:** Puzzle content is the highest-performing format for xiangqi on Douyin. The short, self-contained nature suits short-form video perfectly. The combination of tension (will you solve it?), education (the explanation), and personality (your reaction) hits all engagement drivers.

### Series 3: Live Play with Commentary (实战解说)

**Concept:** Play online games while explaining your thought process in real time. Post the best games as standalone videos or compile highlights.

**Execution tips:**
- Use 慢棋 (standard time) so you have time to talk. Blitz is too fast for live commentary unless you are very experienced at talking while playing.
- Do not only show games you win. Showing losses with honest analysis builds more trust than a highlight reel of victories.
- When facing a strong opponent, narrate the difficulty: "This person is rated 200 points above me, so I need to be very careful in the opening."

### Series 4: "Reviewing Your Games" (观众棋谱点评)

**Concept:** Viewers submit their 天天象棋 game records. You load them into the app and analyze them, pointing out mistakes, missed opportunities, and teaching principles.

**How to collect submissions:**
- Ask viewers to share their game records via Douyin comments or a WeChat group.
- Select 2-3 games per episode with different skill levels represented.
- Use the 智能复盘 engine analysis to support your commentary.

**Why it works:** Viewer-submitted content drives engagement because people watch to see if their game gets featured. It also provides infinite content with zero preparation cost — viewers do the work of playing the game, and you provide the analysis.

## 5. Recording Mobile Gameplay

To turn 天天象棋 gameplay into publishable content, you need to capture your screen cleanly.

### iOS Screen Recording

**Built-in method (simplest):**
1. Open Settings → Control Center → add "Screen Recording" if not already present.
2. Swipe down from the top-right corner to open Control Center.
3. Long-press the Screen Recording button.
4. Select your microphone source (enable microphone for live commentary).
5. Tap "Start Recording." A 3-second countdown begins.
6. Play your game. The red status bar at the top indicates recording is active.
7. To stop: tap the red bar at the top and confirm, or open Control Center and tap the recording button again.
8. The recording saves to your Photos app as an MP4 file.

**Quality settings:**
- iOS records at the device's native resolution (1080p on most modern iPhones).
- Ensure Do Not Disturb is enabled to prevent notification pop-ups during recording.
- Close unnecessary apps to prevent performance drops.

### AirPlay to Mac with OBS (higher quality)

This method gives you more control over recording quality, resolution, and simultaneous camera capture (for facecam overlay).

1. **On your Mac:** Download and install OBS Studio from obsproject.com.
2. **Install an AirPlay receiver:** Use a tool like "AirServer" or the built-in AirPlay receiver on macOS Monterey and later (System Preferences → Sharing → AirPlay Receiver).
3. **On your iPhone:** Open Control Center, tap Screen Mirroring, and select your Mac.
4. **In OBS:** Add a new "Window Capture" source and select the AirPlay receiver window showing your iPhone screen.
5. **Add audio:** In OBS, add an "Audio Input Capture" source for your microphone.
6. **Optional — add facecam:** Add a "Video Capture Device" source for your webcam. Position it in a corner of the scene.
7. **Set output resolution:** Configure OBS to output at 1080x1920 (vertical) for Douyin or 1920x1080 (horizontal) for Bilibili/YouTube.
8. **Record:** Click "Start Recording" in OBS. Play your game on the phone.

**Advantages of the OBS method:**
- You can add overlays (your channel name, current rating, episode number).
- Facecam adds personality and builds connection with viewers.
- Audio quality is better through a dedicated microphone connected to your Mac.
- You can record at higher bitrates for sharper text on the chess board.

### Android Screen Recording

Most Android devices running Android 10 or later have built-in screen recording:

1. Swipe down the notification shade twice to access Quick Settings.
2. Find and tap "Screen Record" (屏幕录制).
3. Choose whether to record audio from the microphone, device audio, or both.
4. Tap "Start." A countdown begins.
5. Play your game.
6. Stop recording from the notification shade.

For devices without built-in recording, install a third-party app like AZ Screen Recorder from your app store.

### Recording best practices for xiangqi content

- **Lock screen orientation.** 天天象棋 is primarily a portrait app. Ensure your recording matches.
- **Increase screen brightness to 80-100%.** This ensures the board and pieces are clearly visible in the recording, even when viewers watch on smaller screens.
- **Increase text size in the app settings if available.** Move notations and player names should be readable in the video.
- **Use airplane mode or Do Not Disturb.** Incoming calls, messages, and notifications during recording are unprofessional and disruptive.
- **Charge your device or plug it in.** Screen recording consumes significant battery. A dying phone mid-game ruins the content.

## 6. Workflow Integration: 天天象棋 with Other Tools

天天象棋 is most powerful when used as one piece of a broader content creation workflow rather than in isolation.

### The recommended workflow

```
Play on 天天象棋
       ↓
Export game record (棋谱)
       ↓
Import into analysis tools
       ↓
Prepare commentary notes
       ↓
Record final video with analysis overlay
       ↓
Edit and publish to Douyin / Bilibili / YouTube
```

### Step-by-step: exporting game records

1. Open 棋谱收藏 (Game Collection) in 天天象棋.
2. Select the game you want to analyze.
3. Tap the share/export button (分享).
4. Options vary by version, but look for:
   - Copy game record text (复制棋谱) — gives you a text-based move list.
   - Share to WeChat/QQ — sends the game as a mini-program link (useful for sharing with collaborators but not for tool import).
5. If the app does not offer direct text export, manually transcribe the move list from the replay screen into standard Chinese notation format.

### Importing into analysis tools

Once you have the game record in text format, you can use it in several ways:

- **Load into a desktop xiangqi engine** (like 象棋巫师 or similar software) for deeper analysis than the app provides. Desktop engines typically offer stronger evaluation and more customization.
- **Use our project tools** to generate board images, annotated diagrams, or position breakdowns that look better on camera than a phone screen recording.
- **Create side-by-side comparisons:** Show the 天天象棋 AI Review on one side and your own deeper analysis on the other. This highlights your added value as a commentator beyond what the app provides automatically.

### How 天天象棋 complements (rather than replaces) your workflow

| Task | 天天象棋 | External Tools |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Playing games | Primary platform — matchmaking, rating | Not needed for playing |
| Quick post-game analysis | 智能复盘 — fast, convenient, good enough for most content | Deeper analysis for complex positions |
| Endgame puzzles | 残局闯关 — 500+ puzzles, self-contained content | Custom puzzle creation from your own material |
| Board visualization | Small screen, limited customization | High-resolution board images, custom annotations, multiple board layouts |
| Game record storage | Automatic, searchable | Organized archives, tags, cross-referencing with analysis notes |
| Content recording | Screen recording — functional but basic | OBS with overlays, facecam, higher production quality |

The ideal approach is: use 天天象棋 as your primary playing and quick analysis platform, then elevate the best content through external tools for higher production quality before publishing.
