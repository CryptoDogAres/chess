# Complete YouTube Channel Setup Guide

A comprehensive, step-by-step guide to building a xiangqi (Chinese chess) channel on YouTube. Covers channel creation, branding, SEO, monetization, and growth strategies with exact specs and ready-to-use templates.

---

## 1. Channel Creation and Branding

### Creating the Channel

1. Go to youtube.com and sign in with a Google account.
2. Click your profile icon → **Create a channel**.
3. Choose **Use a custom name** (recommended for a brand channel): e.g., "XiangqiMaster" or "ChineseChessDaily."
4. A brand account lets multiple people manage the channel without sharing personal Google credentials.

### Channel Name Guidelines

- Keep it under 20 characters for clean display across devices.
- Include "xiangqi," "Chinese chess," or "象棋" for discoverability.
- Suggested formats:
  - `Xiangqi [Name]` — e.g., Xiangqi Academy
  - `[Name] Chinese Chess` — e.g., Wang's Chinese Chess
  - `[Name]象棋` — for bilingual branding

## 2. Profile and Visual Specs

### Channel Icon (Profile Picture)

- **Dimensions:** 800 x 800 px.
- **Display:** Circular crop, 98 x 98 px on most interfaces.
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, BMP, or non-animated GIF.
- **File size:** Under 4 MB.
- Use a clean, recognizable image. A chess piece logo or headshot with high contrast works best at small sizes.

### Channel Banner (Banner Art)

- **Upload dimensions:** 2560 x 1440 px.
- **Safe area for all devices:** 1546 x 423 px (center of the image).
- **Desktop display:** 2560 x 423 px.
- **Mobile display:** 1546 x 423 px.
- **TV display:** Full 2560 x 1440 px.
- **Format:** JPG or PNG, under 6 MB.

Place your channel name, tagline, and posting schedule within the 1546 x 423 center zone.

### Video Watermark (Branding Watermark)

- **Dimensions:** 150 x 150 px.
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background.
- Appears in the bottom-right corner of all videos. Use a simple logo or subscribe icon.
- Set in: YouTube Studio → Customization → Branding → Video watermark.

## 4. Playlist Organization Strategy

Organize content into playlists from day one. This increases session watch time and makes your channel navigable.

### Recommended Playlist Structure

1. **Beginner Tutorials** — Rules, piece movements, basic checkmates, first opening principles.
2. **Opening Systems** — One playlist per major opening (中炮 vs 屏风马, 飞相局, 仙人指路, etc.) or a single "Openings" playlist.
3. **Endgame Puzzles** — Short puzzle videos, organized by difficulty or piece combination.
4. **Full Game Analyses** — Complete game commentaries from professional or personal games.
5. **Tournament Coverage** — Event-specific playlists (e.g., "2026 National Xiangqi Championship").
6. **Live Stream Replays** — Archived live sessions.
7. **Shorts** — All short-form vertical content.
8. **Series** — Any multi-part series (e.g., "30 Days to Xiangqi Competence").

Set your **Featured Sections** on the channel homepage to display 2–4 key playlists for new visitors.

### Thumbnail Specs

- **Dimensions:** 1280 x 720 px (16:9 ratio).
- **File size:** Under 2 MB.
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or GIF (non-animated).
- Use bold text (3–5 words max), a face/reaction shot or dramatic board position, and high contrast colors. Avoid clutter.

## 6. YouTube SEO

### Title Formula

```
[Primary Keyword] — [Specific Detail] | [Hook/Benefit]
```

Examples:
- "Xiangqi Opening Trap — Central Cannon Double Horseback Attack | Win in 15 Moves"
- "Chinese Chess Endgame Puzzle #47 — Can You Find the Mate in 3?"
- "象棋残局: Chariot + Horse vs Chariot Endgame Explained"

**Rules:**
- Keep titles under 60 characters (longer titles get truncated in search results and feeds).
- Front-load the primary keyword ("Xiangqi," "Chinese Chess," or the specific topic).
- Include a number or specific claim when possible.
- Do not use ALL CAPS for more than one word.

## 7. Thumbnail A/B Testing (Test & Compare)

YouTube's built-in thumbnail testing feature (available to channels in the YouTube Partner Program):

1. Upload your video with the original thumbnail.
2. Go to YouTube Studio → Content → select the video → Details → Thumbnail section.
3. Click **Test & Compare**.
4. Upload 2–3 thumbnail variants.
5. YouTube will distribute each variant to equal audience segments and report which one generates the highest click-through rate.
6. After sufficient data (usually 1–2 weeks), YouTube automatically selects the winner or you can choose manually.

**Testing strategy for xiangqi content:**
- Variant A: Board position close-up with bold text ("IMPOSSIBLE PUZZLE?").
- Variant B: Your face/reaction + smaller board image.
- Variant C: Clean board diagram with colored arrows showing the key move.

## 8. YouTube Partner Program (Monetization)

### Requirements

- 1,000 subscribers.
- 4,000 valid public watch hours in the past 12 months OR 10 million valid public Shorts views in the past 90 days.
- Linked AdSense account.
- No active community guideline strikes.
- Two-step verification enabled on Google account.

### Revenue Streams Once Accepted

1. **Ad revenue:** Pre-roll, mid-roll (for videos 8+ minutes), and post-roll ads.
2. **Channel memberships:** Monthly paid subscriptions with custom badges and perks (requires 1,000+ subs).
3. **Super Chat / Super Stickers:** Paid highlighted messages during live streams.
4. **Super Thanks:** One-time tips on published videos.
5. **Merchandise shelf:** Link to approved merch stores (requires 1,000+ subs).

### Maximizing Ad Revenue

- Videos 8+ minutes can have mid-roll ads. Place them at natural breaks (between analysis segments).
- Longer watch time = more ad slots. Structure content to maintain engagement through the full duration.
- CPM for education/knowledge content typically ranges from $2–8 depending on audience geography.

## 9. Shorts Strategy

Repurpose Douyin/TikTok vertical content directly as YouTube Shorts.

### Specs

- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16).
- **Duration:** Up to 3 minutes. Under 60 seconds recommended for maximum distribution.
- **Upload:** Upload as a regular video; YouTube auto-detects vertical format and classifies as a Short. Alternatively, use the Shorts camera in the YouTube mobile app.
- Add `#Shorts` in the title or description for reliable classification.

### Content Ideas for Shorts

- Daily xiangqi puzzles (show position, ask "what is the best move?", reveal answer).
- "One move that changed the game" — isolate a single critical moment from a long analysis.
- Quick opening traps in under 30 seconds.
- "Guess the next move" interactive format.

### Repurposing Workflow

1. Create the original video for Douyin (9:16, under 60 seconds).
2. Remove any Douyin watermark (re-export from 剪映/CapCut).
3. Upload to YouTube as a Short.
4. Add English title and description for the YouTube audience.

## 10. Community Tab

Available to all channels (previously required 500 subs, now available by default).

### Usage Ideas

- **Polls:** "Which opening should I cover next?" with 4 options. Polls get high engagement.
- **Puzzle images:** Post a board position screenshot and ask "White to move — what is the best move? Answer in the comments."
- **Behind-the-scenes updates:** "Currently analyzing [game] for this week's video."
- **New video announcements:** Share when a new upload goes live.
- **Cross-promotion:** Share links to your Bilibili or Douyin content for bilingual audiences.

Post to the community tab 2–3 times per week between video uploads to keep engagement steady.

## 11. End Screens and Cards

### End Screens

- Available in the last 5–20 seconds of any video longer than 25 seconds.
- Add up to 4 elements:
  1. **Subscribe button** (always include).
  2. **Best for viewer** (YouTube auto-selects a recommended video).
  3. **Specific video or playlist** (link to the most relevant next video).
  4. **Channel link** (your own or a collaborator's).
- Design your video outro to leave space for end screen elements. A common layout: right side for video recommendations, left side for subscribe prompt.

### Cards

- Up to 5 cards per video.
- Types: video card, playlist card, channel card, link card (for YPP members).
- Place a card at moments when you mention related content: "I covered this opening in detail — link in the card above."
- Track card click-through rates in YouTube Studio → Analytics → Engagement.

## 12. Recommended Tools

### vidIQ

- Browser extension for YouTube SEO.
- Features: keyword score, competition analysis, tag recommendations, trending alerts.
- Free tier is sufficient for keyword research. Pro ($7.50/mo) adds competitor tracking.
- **Recommended settings:** Enable the sidebar score on every video page; use the keyword research tool before titling each video.

### TubeBuddy

- Browser extension and mobile app.
- Features: tag explorer, A/B test titles/thumbnails, bulk processing, publish scheduling.
- Free tier available. Pro ($2.80/mo) adds tag suggestions and basic A/B testing.
- **Recommended settings:** Enable Tag Explorer for every upload; use the SEO Studio checklist during publishing.

### Using Both Together

- Use vidIQ for keyword/topic research before creating content.
- Use TubeBuddy for the publishing workflow and optimization checklist.
- Both integrate directly into YouTube Studio.

## 13. Growth Strategies

### Collaborate with Other Xiangqi/Chess Creators

- Search YouTube for "xiangqi," "Chinese chess," "象棋" and find channels of similar size.
- Propose collaboration formats: puzzle challenges, game reviews, or cross-analysis of the same game.
- Collaboration exposes both channels to new audiences.

### Leverage YouTube Search

- YouTube is the second-largest search engine. Xiangqi is a niche with relatively low competition in English.
- Target specific long-tail queries: "how to play xiangqi for beginners," "xiangqi opening strategy," "Chinese chess endgame chariot vs horse."
- Use YouTube autocomplete to find what people search for.

### Create Evergreen Content

- Tutorials and guides get consistent search traffic for years.
- Prioritize "how to play xiangqi" and "xiangqi rules" videos early — they become permanent traffic sources.

### Engage with Comments

- Reply to every comment on every video, especially in the first 48 hours.
- Heart comments to increase the commenter's loyalty.
- Pin a comment with a question to spark discussion.
