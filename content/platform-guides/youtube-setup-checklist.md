# YouTube Channel Complete Setup Checklist

A step-by-step guide to setting up your xiangqi YouTube channel from scratch, with exact instructions for every setting, branding element, and content structure.

---

## 1. Channel Creation

### Step-by-Step Account Setup

1. Go to [youtube.com](https://youtube.com) and sign in with your Google account
2. Click your profile icon in the top right corner
3. Click "Create a channel"
4. Choose "Use a custom name" (do NOT use your personal Google account name)
5. Enter your channel name (see naming suggestions below)
6. Click "Create"

### Channel Name Suggestions

Your channel name should communicate the content topic and be memorable. Consider these options:

| Name | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| 象棋ChessLab | Bilingual, modern feel, recognizable | "Lab" is overused |
| XiangqiMaster | Clear, SEO-friendly | Generic |
| 棋道 ChessWay | Philosophical, elegant | Less obvious for new viewers |
| 象棋局中局 | Intriguing Chinese name, suggests depth | Not searchable in English |
| Xiangqi Decoded | English-first, implies analysis | Misses Chinese-speaking audience |

**Recommended approach**: Use a bilingual name that includes "象棋" for Chinese-speaking viewers and "Xiangqi" or "Chinese Chess" for English-speaking viewers. Match whatever name you use on Douyin and Bilibili for cross-platform recognition.

### Channel Handle

Your handle (@username) appears in your channel URL and makes it easy for people to find you.

- Go to YouTube Studio → Customization → Basic info → Handle
- Try: @xiangqi, @chinesechess, @xiangqimaster, @xiangqi[yourname]
- If your preferred handle is taken, add a descriptor: @xiangqipuzzles, @xiangqianalysis
- Keep it short, lowercase, no special characters
- This handle should match your handles on other platforms if possible

## 2. Branding

Consistent visual branding makes your channel look professional and helps viewers recognize your content across platforms.

### Profile Picture (800 x 800 pixels)

Your profile picture appears next to every video, comment, and in search results. It must be recognizable at small sizes.

**What to use**:
- A logo featuring a xiangqi piece (the general/帅 or cannon/炮 are most visually distinctive)
- If you appear on camera, a professional headshot with a xiangqi board in the background
- High contrast colors that stand out against YouTube's white/dark backgrounds

**Technical specs**:
- Size: 800 x 800 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: under 4MB
- If using branding.html outputs, export the logo at 800x800

**To upload**: YouTube Studio → Customization → Branding → Picture → Upload

### Banner Image (2560 x 1440 pixels)

The banner is the large image at the top of your channel page. It displays differently on desktop, mobile, and TV, so design for the "safe area."

**Safe area**: The center 1546 x 423 pixels will be visible on ALL devices. Keep your important text and imagery within this zone.

**What to include in the banner**:
- Channel name in large text (bilingual)
- A brief tagline: "象棋教学 | 名局解说 | 残局挑战" or "Xiangqi Analysis & Puzzles"
- Upload schedule: "New videos every Tuesday & Friday" (only if you can commit to it)
- Visual elements: xiangqi board background, piece illustrations, your brand colors

**Technical specs**:
- Size: 2560 x 1440 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: under 6MB
- If using branding.html outputs, export the banner at 2560x1440

**To upload**: YouTube Studio → Customization → Branding → Banner image → Upload

### Channel Trailer

A channel trailer plays automatically for non-subscribers who visit your channel page. It should be 30-60 seconds long and answer three questions: Who are you? What do you make? Why should I subscribe?

**Trailer script template** (60 seconds):

```
[0-5s] Open on an exciting xiangqi position. A dramatic sacrifice plays out.

[5-15s] "Welcome to [Channel Name]. This channel is dedicated to the
art and strategy of xiangqi — Chinese chess."

[15-30s] Quick montage: puzzle clips, game analysis moments,
tutorial snippets. Show the variety of your content.

[30-45s] "Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced player,
you'll find puzzles to challenge you, famous games to study,
and strategies to improve your game."

[45-55s] "New videos every week. Subscribe and join
the xiangqi community."

[55-60s] End with your logo/branding and a subscribe button animation.
```

**To set the trailer**: YouTube Studio → Customization → Layout → Channel trailer for people who haven't subscribed → Select your trailer video

## 3. Playlist Setup

Playlists organize your content, improve watch time (YouTube auto-plays the next video in a playlist), and make your channel page look structured and professional.

### Create These 7 Playlists

Go to YouTube Studio → Playlists → New Playlist for each one.

#### Playlist 1: 残局谜题 Endgame Puzzles
- **Description**: "Xiangqi endgame puzzles to test your skills. Pause the video, find the winning move, then check the answer. New puzzles every week. 每周更新象棋残局谜题。"
- **Content**: All puzzle videos, organized from easier to harder
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 2: 名局解说 Famous Game Analysis
- **Description**: "Deep analysis of famous xiangqi games from championship tournaments and legendary players. Learn from the best games in xiangqi history. 经典名局深度解说。"
- **Content**: Full game analysis videos
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 3: 新手教程 Beginner Tutorials
- **Description**: "Learn xiangqi from scratch. Rules, piece movements, basic openings, and fundamental strategies. Perfect for beginners. 象棋零基础入门教程。"
- **Content**: All tutorial content, ordered from most basic to advanced
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 4: 这步你会走吗 Would You Play This?
- **Description**: "Interactive xiangqi challenges. I show you the position — you decide the move. Comment your answer before watching the solution. 互动象棋挑战。"
- **Content**: Interactive format videos
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 5: 引擎vs大师 Engine vs Grandmaster
- **Description**: "What happens when we analyze grandmaster moves with modern engines? Sometimes the human is better. 当AI遇上象棋大师。"
- **Content**: Engine analysis comparison videos
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 6: 直播精华 Live Stream Highlights
- **Description**: "Best moments from my live streams. Analysis, puzzles solved in real-time, and audience challenges. 直播精彩片段合集。"
- **Content**: Clips from live streams
- **Visibility**: Public

#### Playlist 7: Shorts 短视频集
- **Description**: "Quick xiangqi puzzles, tips, and highlights. 60 seconds or less. 象棋短视频合集。"
- **Content**: All Shorts/vertical videos
- **Visibility**: Public

### Playlist Tips

- Order videos within each playlist intentionally. For tutorials, go beginner to advanced. For puzzles, go easy to hard.
- Add new videos to the appropriate playlist as soon as you upload them. Do not let videos sit unorganized.
- Feature your 2-3 best playlists on your channel home page (YouTube Studio → Customization → Layout → Featured sections).

## 5. Default Upload Settings

Setting default upload values saves you time on every upload. These settings apply automatically to new uploads, though you can override them per video.

### Default Description Template

Go to YouTube Studio → Settings → Upload defaults → Description

```
Subscribe for more xiangqi content: [your channel URL]
订阅频道获取更多象棋内容

🔗 Other platforms:
Douyin/抖音: [handle]
Bilibili/B站: [handle]

📋 Playlists:
Endgame Puzzles 残局谜题: [playlist URL]
Famous Games 名局解说: [playlist URL]
Beginner Tutorials 新手教程: [playlist URL]

#xiangqi #chinesechess #象棋 #chesspuzzle #chessstrategy
```

This template appears automatically in every new upload's description. Add video-specific text above it.

### Default Tags

Go to YouTube Studio → Settings → Upload defaults → Tags

Add these default tags (they apply to every video unless you remove them):
```
xiangqi, chinese chess, 象棋, chess puzzle, xiangqi puzzle,
xiangqi strategy, chinese chess tutorial, 象棋教学,
xiangqi analysis, chess, board game, strategy game,
xiangqi opening, xiangqi endgame, 残局, 中国象棋
```

### Default Visibility

Set to "Private" or "Unlisted" by default. This prevents accidental publishing of unfinished uploads. You manually set each video to "Public" or schedule it when ready.

### End Screen Template

End screens appear in the last 20 seconds of your video and can display:
- A subscribe button
- A link to another video
- A link to a playlist

**Recommended end screen layout**:
- Left side: "Best for viewer" video recommendation (YouTube picks the most relevant video)
- Right side: Subscribe button
- Bottom: Link to your most popular playlist

You cannot set a default end screen in upload settings, but you can import from a previous video. Create one video with a perfect end screen, then use "Import from video" for all future uploads.

### Cards

Cards are clickable links that appear during your video. Use them to link to related content.

**When to add a card**:
- When you mention another video: "I analyzed this opening in detail in another video" → add a card linking to that video
- When you reference a playlist: "Check out my beginner tutorial playlist" → add a card
- At a natural break point: midway through a long video, add a card to a related video to keep the viewer in your content ecosystem

## 6. Verification and Feature Activation

New YouTube channels have limited features. Verify your account to unlock everything you need.

### Phone Verification

1. Go to youtube.com/verify
2. Enter your phone number
3. Receive a verification code via text or call
4. Enter the code

**Unlocks**: Videos longer than 15 minutes, custom thumbnails, live streaming, appeals for Content ID claims.

### Custom Thumbnails

After phone verification, you can upload custom thumbnails for every video. This is critical for performance.

**Thumbnail guidelines for xiangqi content**:
- Size: 1280 x 720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Format: JPG, GIF, or PNG
- Max file size: 2MB
- Include: a clear board position, 2-4 words of text, high contrast
- Avoid: tiny text, cluttered images, misleading content
- Be consistent: use the same color scheme, font, and layout style across thumbnails

### External Links

After reaching 500 subscribers, you can add external links to your channel page.

**Links to add**:
- Douyin profile
- Bilibili profile
- Discord server (if you create one for your community)
- Any merchandise or Patreon page (future)

**To add**: YouTube Studio → Customization → Basic info → Links

### YouTube Partner Program (YPP)

To monetize your channel, you need to meet YPP requirements:
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months OR 10 million Shorts views in the past 90 days
- Follow YouTube monetization policies
- Have an AdSense account linked

**When to apply**: As soon as you meet the thresholds. YouTube will review your channel. Approval typically takes 1-4 weeks.

**What YPP unlocks**: Ad revenue, Super Chat, channel memberships, YouTube Shopping features.
