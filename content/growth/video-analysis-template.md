# Top 10 Video Analysis Template

**Purpose:** After your first month of publishing, use this template to systematically analyze your best-performing videos, extract patterns, and make data-driven decisions for the next month.

**Task completed:** ga8 (top video analysis framework)

---

## When to Use This Template

- **First analysis:** 30 days after your first video upload
- **Ongoing:** Repeat on the 1st of every month
- **Data window:** Use 30-day cumulative data for each video
- **Minimum sample:** You need at least 10 published videos for this analysis to be meaningful. If you have fewer, wait until you do.

**Common reasons for underperformance:**
- Weak hook (high impression count but low CTR)
- Too long for the platform (high views but very low completion)
- Topic too niche (low impressions -- algorithm did not push it)
- Poor thumbnail (low CTR compared to similar videos)
- Uploaded at bad time (check day of week and time)
- Audio/video quality issues

## Quick Reference: Where to Find Each Metric

### Douyin (抖音)
- Path: Creator Center (创作者中心) > Content Analysis (内容分析) > Select Video
- Key fields: 播放量 (views), 完播率 (completion), 平均播放时长, 点赞数, 评论数, 分享数

### Bilibili (B站)
- Path: Creator Center (创作中心) > Data Center (数据中心) > Video Data (稿件数据)
- Key fields: 播放量, 播放完成率, 平均观看时长, 点赞, 投币, 收藏, 弹幕, 评论

### YouTube
- Path: YouTube Studio > Analytics > Content > Select Video
- Key fields: Views, Average view duration, Audience retention (%), Likes, Comments, Subscribers gained
