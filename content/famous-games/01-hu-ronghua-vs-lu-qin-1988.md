## 总结与启示 Summary & Lessons (24:00-26:00)

好，我们来总结一下今天这盘棋教给我们的东西。
(Alright, let's summarize what this game teaches us.)

**第一，不要急于进攻。**
(First, don't rush to attack.)
胡荣华开局非常稳健，先把子力调配好，再找机会。很多朋友下棋喜欢一上来就冲杀，结果阵型散了，反而被对方抓住机会。
(Hu Ronghua's opening was very steady — first coordinate your pieces, then look for opportunities. Many players like to charge in from the start, but their formation falls apart, and the opponent seizes the chance.)

**第二，每步棋要有多重功能。**
(Second, every move should serve multiple purposes.)
还记得那步马四进六吗？一匹马做了三件事。下棋的时候，想一想你的这步棋除了表面的目的，还能做什么别的事情。
(Remember that horse leap to the 6th file? One horse accomplished three things. When you play, think about what else your move can accomplish beyond its obvious purpose.)

**第三，不要贪吃子。**
(Third, don't be greedy with captures.)
胡荣华有好几次可以吃子的机会，但他都忍住了。他看的是更大的图景——进攻的节奏、子力的配合、最终的杀棋。
(Hu Ronghua had several chances to capture material, but he held back each time. He was looking at the bigger picture — the rhythm of attack, the coordination of pieces, the eventual checkmate.)

**第四，尊重经验的力量。**
(Fourth, respect the power of experience.)
吕钦的棋下得并不差，但胡荣华就是多了那份沉稳和老练。有时候，比你厉害的人不是因为他算得更远，而是因为他见过更多的局面，知道什么时候该忍，什么时候该出手。
(Lu Qin didn't play badly, but Hu Ronghua had that extra composure and seasoning. Sometimes, stronger players aren't better because they calculate deeper, but because they've seen more positions and know when to hold back and when to strike.)
