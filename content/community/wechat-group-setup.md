# WeChat Group Setup Guide for Xiangqi Channel

**Purpose:** Create an exclusive, high-value WeChat community for the most engaged viewers. WeChat groups are more personal and intimate than QQ groups, making them ideal for a VIP tier.

**Task completed:** cc7 (WeChat community setup)

---

## 1. Create the WeChat Group

**Step-by-step:**

1. Open WeChat, tap "+" in the top right
2. Select "New Group Chat"
3. Add at least one person to start (you can add a secondary account or a friend who will help moderate)
4. Set the group name: **棋道VIP**
   - Alternative names: "棋道象棋VIP群", "棋道内部交流"
5. Set group avatar: Channel logo with a VIP badge or gold accent to distinguish from the free QQ group
6. Enable "Group QR Code" for controlled invitations

**Important WeChat group limits:**
- Groups under 100 members: anyone with the QR code can join
- Groups 100-200 members: existing members can invite, but new members must be confirmed by the inviter
- Groups 200-500 members: new members must be confirmed by the group owner
- **Maximum capacity: 500 members** (hard limit, no upgrades available)

## 2. Gating Strategy: VIP Access Only

This group is NOT open to everyone. It is a reward for your most engaged viewers. This exclusivity creates real value and motivates deeper engagement with your content.

### Who Gets Access

**Qualification criteria (meet ANY one):**

| Criteria | How to Verify |
|----------|---------------|
| Watched 10+ videos | Ask them to screenshot their watch history or name recent videos |
| Tipped/donated on any platform | Screenshot of tip confirmation |
| Bilibili annual/monthly VIP subscriber | Bilibili membership screenshot |
| YouTube channel member | YouTube membership screenshot |
| Won a weekly puzzle challenge in QQ group | Admin confirms from QQ group records |
| Purchased merch or course (future) | Order confirmation |

### Gating Process

**Step 1: Announce in videos and QQ group**

Script for video:
```
我们有一个VIP微信群，只对铁粉开放。
怎么加入？只要你看过我10个以上的视频，或者在任何平台打赏过，
就可以私信我截图证明，我拉你进群。
群里有专属内容、提前看视频、还能直接问我问题。
名额有限，先到先得！
```

**Step 2: Application process**

Direct viewers to send a private WeChat message (or QQ message / platform DM) with:
1. Their name or platform username
2. Proof of qualification (screenshot)
3. A brief intro: how long they've played xiangqi

**Step 3: Manual approval**

You (or your admin) verify the proof and add them to the group. This manual step is intentional -- it reinforces the VIP feeling and keeps quality high.

### QR Code Management

- Generate a new QR code every 2 weeks
- Only share the QR code through DMs to verified applicants
- Never post the QR code publicly in videos or open groups
- When group exceeds 100 members, QR code stops working -- switch to direct invitations only

## 5. Managing Growth (500-Member Limit)

WeChat's 500-member hard limit means you must plan carefully.

### Phase 1: Building (0-100 members)

- Actively recruit qualified viewers
- Be very present in the group yourself -- reply to messages daily
- Seed content consistently to establish the group's value
- Goal timeline: first 100 members within 3 months

### Phase 2: Growing (100-300 members)

- Tighten admission criteria slightly if demand increases
- Appoint 1-2 trusted members as informal moderators
- Establish a consistent content posting rhythm
- Monitor group activity: if engagement drops, increase exclusive content

### Phase 3: Approaching Capacity (300-500 members)

- Raise the bar for admission (e.g., require tip/donation, not just video count)
- Consider creating tiered access:
  - Main VIP group (500 cap) for most engaged
  - Overflow group "棋道VIP二群" for additional qualified members
- Begin transitioning broader announcements to WeChat Official Account

### Phase 4: At Capacity (500 members)

- Implement a "one in, one out" policy for truly inactive members
  - Inactive = no messages in 60 days AND no reaction to polls
  - Message inactive members privately before removing
- Use WeChat Official Account for content that all VIPs should see
- Reserve the group for discussion and interaction only

### Handling Multiple Groups

If you need more than 500 VIP members:
- 棋道VIP (main, most active members)
- 棋道VIP二群 (overflow, still valuable but slightly less exclusive)
- Cross-post important announcements to both
- Keep Q&A sessions in the main group only

## 6. Backup: WeChat Official Account (公众号)

A WeChat Official Account complements the group by providing broadcast capability without the 500-member limit.

### Why You Need Both

| Feature | WeChat Group | Official Account |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Member limit | 500 | Unlimited |
| Interaction | Real-time chat | Articles + comments |
| Content format | Messages, images, short clips | Long-form articles, embedded video |
| Discoverability | Invite only | Searchable on WeChat |
| Push notifications | Every message notifies | 1 push per day (subscription) or 4/month (service) |

### Setting Up the Official Account

1. Go to mp.weixin.qq.com
2. Register a Subscription Account (订阅号) -- best for content creators
3. Account name: **棋道象棋** or **棋道Xiangqi**
4. Verify with your ID (required for all official accounts in China)

### Content Strategy for Official Account

| Frequency | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| 2x per week | Article version of your video content (with embedded video link) |
| 1x per week | Exclusive written analysis or xiangqi culture essay |
| 1x per month | "Best of" compilation from VIP group discussions |
| As needed | Announcements, event promotions, milestone celebrations |
