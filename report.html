教学方法论 Teaching Methodology — How the Best Explain Moves

许银川模式 — The Xu Yinchuan Gold Standard

1

No Jargon 去专业术语

Says "my cannon moves next to the chariot" (我的炮移到车旁边) instead of "炮三平六". Makes analysis accessible to all levels.

2

Prepared Courseware 备课制

Structured like a 百家讲坛 episode. Not improvised rambling — planned lesson content with clear objectives.

3

Interactive Pauses 互动暂停

"What would you play here?" — pauses for viewer engagement before revealing the correct continuation.

4

Variation Trees 变化树

Shows the main line (best play), then 2-3 inferior alternatives explaining why they fail within 3-5 moves.

5

Honest About Mistakes 坦诚失误

Acknowledges uncertainty during live play. "I'm not sure here..." builds trust and relatability.

通用解说框架 — Universal Commentary Framework

坏棋示范 Bad Move Explanation

  • Show the tempting but wrong move first
  • Play out 3-5 moves of the losing continuation
  • Highlight the specific moment it goes wrong
  • Explain the principle violated (tempo loss, piece trapped, king exposed)
  • "If you play the horse here... look what happens after cannon pins..."

好棋示范 Good Move Explanation

  • Reveal the correct move after showing failures
  • Show 5-10 moves of the winning main line
  • Explain why it works (tactical motif, positional advantage)
  • Connect to a general principle: 节奏 tempo, 空间控制 space, 阵型 formation
  • "The cannon sacrifice opens the file because..."

解说中如何使用引擎 — How Commentators Use Engines

  • Real-time eval display — Engine evaluation score and principal variation visible on screen during recording/streaming
  • "Engine line" commentary — Show the engine's suggested best move vs. the move actually played, then explain the divergence
  • Post-game review — 天天象棋 and xiangqi.com offer move-by-move quality assessments (brilliant/good/mistake/blunder)
  • Multiple engine cross-check — Advanced commentators run 2 engines simultaneously to validate evaluations
  • Position search via ChessDB — Interactive position lookup with win/draw/loss statistics for any position
Recommendation 推荐 Start with Pikafish (free, Elo 3951, just 2 points below the commercial leader). Load it into 飞将象棋 or 象棋巫师 via UCCI protocol. This gives you world-class analysis for zero cost.

算法与SEO优化 — Algorithm & SEO Optimization

  • Reply to 50+ comments within 2 hours of posting — channels that do this see 15-20% higher algorithmic reach
  • Titles under 60 characters — curiosity-driven and benefit-focused. "3 Endgame Mistakes Every Beginner Makes" beats "Xiangqi Endgame Tutorial 2026"
  • 90% of top videos use custom thumbnails — bold text, contrasting colors, dramatic board position close-up
  • First 3 seconds must hook — content must deliver on the title's promise immediately or retention drops, killing algorithmic distribution
  • Bilibili: Cover image with large bold text (封面大粗字) is critical; first 8 characters of title must hook
  • Post around 5:30 PM on Bilibili for optimal evening traffic window (after content review clears)
  • YouTube A/B test thumbnails — use Test & Compare feature with up to 3 variants per video

蓝海机会 Underserved Niches & Differentiation

English-Language Xiangqi

Blue Ocean

Near-zero competition. 1.2B players globally but virtually no English content. The "xqinenglish" channel is basically alone. Huge untapped diaspora + curious international chess players.

Bilingual 中英双语 Content

High Potential

Bridge Chinese and international chess communities. Chinese narration with English subtitles (or vice versa). Appeals to heritage learners and language students simultaneously.

"Xiangqi for Chess Players"

Validated Interest

ChessBase has published on this angle. International chess players curious about xiangqi = built-in audience from the world's largest chess community. "How xiangqi improves your chess."

High Production Quality

Easy Differentiation

Most xiangqi content has low production values vs. international chess. Professional graphics, board animations, and polished editing would immediately stand out in the space.

AI-Powered Analysis Content

Trending

Using Pikafish evaluations the way GothamChess uses Stockfish. "Engine says this is a blunder — here's why." Move quality grades, accuracy scores, game trend charts.

Structured Learning Paths

Gap in Market

No "Chessable for xiangqi" exists. Progressive beginner → intermediate → advanced courses with spaced repetition. First mover advantage available.

Cultural/Historical Storytelling

Unique Angle

Famous games as dramatic narratives, not just technical analysis. Connect to Chinese history, poetry (《适情雅趣》), and legendary rivalries. Content that non-players can enjoy.

Cross-Variant Comparisons

Novel

Comparing xiangqi, shogi, and international chess strategies. "What does this tactic look like in each game?" Appeals to the broader board game community.
